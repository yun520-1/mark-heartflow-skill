### 1. Meta-Agency Theory | 元能动性理论

**Source | 来源**: SEP Agency, Frankfurt (1971), Watson (1975), Bratman (1987), Searle (1990)

**English:**
Integrates hierarchical models of agency, from basic intentional action to reflective volitional control to social and normative agency. Key innovations include Frankfurt Cases assessment for moral responsibility and planning theory for temporal extension of agency.

整合理想层级模型，从基本意向行动到反思意志控制再到社会和规范能动性。关键创新包括用于道德责任的 Frankfurt 案例评估和用于能动性时间延伸的规划理论。

- First-order desires vs. second-order volitions (Frankfurt)
- Agency spectrum (Watson)

**Key Concepts | 关键概念**:
- Parity principle (internal/external equivalence)
- Cognitive artifacts (tools, technologies)
- Environmental scaffolding
