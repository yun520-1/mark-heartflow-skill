- **为我性形式化**：主观给定性质量的计算建模

---

### 👥 Collective Emotion Deepening | 集体情绪深化

**English:**
- **Four-Layer Shared Experience Model**: Walther's conditions for genuine collective emotion
  - Layer 1: Mutual awareness of shared situation
  - Layer 2: Reciprocal recognition of co-experiencing
  - Layer 3: Joint attention to emotional object
  - Layer 4: Felt sense of "we-experiencing"
- **We-Intention Detection**: Enhanced algorithms for identifying collective intentions
- **Emotional Contagion vs. Collective Emotion**: Refined distinction

**中文:**
- **四层共享体验模型**：瓦尔特的真正集体情绪条件
  - 第一层：对共享情境的相互意识
  - 第二层：对共同体验的互惠性认可
  - 第三层：对情绪对象的联合注意
  - 第四层：共同"我们体验"的感受
- **我们意向检测**：增强的集体意向识别算法
- **情绪感染与集体情绪**：精细化区分

---

### ⏰ Temporal Processing Refinement | 时间处理优化

**English:**
- **12-Level Temporal Depth Model**: Extended from 9 levels
  - Levels 1-3: Immediate present (specious present)
  - Levels 4-6: Recent past/near future (minutes to hours)
  - Levels 7-9: Extended past/medium future (days to weeks)
  - Levels 10-12: Life narrative/existential future (months to years)
- **Temporal Window of Integration (TWI) Calibration**: Individual difference parameters
- **Predictive Temporal Binding**: Forward models for emotional experience structure

**中文:**
- **12 层时间深度模型**：从 9 层扩展
  - 1-3 层：即时现在（显似现在）
  - 4-6 层：近期过去/近期未来（分钟至小时）
  - 7-9 层：扩展过去/中期未来（天至周）
  - 10-12 层：生命叙事/存在未来（月至年）
- **整合时间窗（TWI）校准**：个体差异参数
- **预测性时间绑定**：情绪体验结构的前向模型
