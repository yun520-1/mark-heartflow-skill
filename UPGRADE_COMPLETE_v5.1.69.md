## New Capabilities | 新增能力

### 1. Cognitive Science Integration Framework v1.0 | 认知科学整合框架 v1.0

**English:**
Comprehensive integration of cognitive science's six core disciplines within a single computational framework. Enables cross-disciplinary theory validation, multi-level analysis (computational, algorithmic, implementational), empirical grounding for philosophical theories, and philosophical rigor for empirical models.

**中文:**
在单一计算框架内全面整合认知科学的六个核心学科。支持跨学科理论验证、多层次分析（计算、算法、实现）、哲学理论的实证基础，以及实证模型的哲学严谨性。

### 2. Embodied Cognition 4E Framework v2.0 | 具身认知 4E 框架 v2.0

**English:**
Advanced integration of 4E cognition covering Embodied (body shapes cognition), Embedded (cognition in context), Enactive (cognition as action), and Extended (mind beyond skull) dimensions. Grounded in Merleau-Ponty's phenomenology, Gibson's ecological psychology, and Varela's enactivism.

**中文:**
高级整合 4E 认知，涵盖具身的（身体塑造认知）、嵌入的（情境中的认知）、生成的（认知即行动）和延展的（心智延展出颅骨）维度。基于梅洛 - 庞蒂的现象学、吉布森的生态心理学和瓦雷拉的生成主义。

### 3. Connectionism & Neural Networks v1.0 | 联结主义与神经网络 v1.0

**English:**
Integration of parallel distributed processing, neural network architectures, learning algorithms (backpropagation, Hebbian), and cognitive modeling. Bridges symbolic AI with sub-symbolic processing for hybrid cognitive architectures.

**中文:**
整合并行分布式处理、神经网络架构、学习算法（反向传播、赫布学习）和认知建模。桥接符号 AI 与亚符号处理，实现混合认知架构。

### 4. Dynamical Systems Cognition v1.0 | 动力系统认知 v1.0

**English:**
Integration of attractor dynamics, phase transitions, self-organization, emergence theory, and non-linear dynamics. Provides mathematical framework for understanding cognitive processes as continuous temporal evolution.

**中文:**
整合吸引子动力学、相变、自组织、涌现理论和非线性动力学。为理解认知过程作为连续时间演化提供数学框架。

### 5. Ecological Psychology & Affordances v1.0 | 生态心理学与可供性 v1.0

**English:**
Integration of Gibson's affordance perception, direct perception, invariant detection, optic flow analysis, and animal-environment mutuality. Extends embodied cognition with ecological grounding.

**中文:**
整合吉布森的可供性感知、直接感知、不变量检测、光流分析和动物 - 环境相互性。用生态基础扩展具身认知。
