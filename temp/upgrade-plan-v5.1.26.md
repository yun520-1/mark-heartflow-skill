## Theoretical Foundations | 理论基础

### 1. Predictive Processing & Active Inference | 预测加工与主动推理

**Source | 来源**: SEP Predictive Processing (2024); Friston (2010, 2023); Clark (2013, 2023); Seth (2021)

**English:**

Predictive processing posits that the brain is a hierarchical prediction machine that continuously generates top-down predictions about sensory inputs and updates its models based on prediction errors. Key principles:

1. **Hierarchical Generative Models**: Multi-level predictions from abstract concepts to concrete sensations
2. **Prediction Error Minimization**: The brain minimizes prediction error through:
   - **Perceptual inference**: Updating models to fit sensory data
   - **Active inference**: Acting on the world to make sensory data fit predictions
3. **Free Energy Principle**: Biological systems resist disorder by minimizing variational free energy (upper bound on surprise)
4. **Precision Weighting**: Attention modulates the precision (reliability) assigned to prediction errors

**Emotional Applications**:
- Emotions as predictions about bodily states (interoceptive predictions)
- Anxiety as heightened precision on threat-related prediction errors
- Depression as altered priors about self-efficacy and reward
- Emotion regulation as precision modulation or prior updating

**中文:**

预测加工认为大脑是一个层级预测机器，持续生成关于感官输入的自上而下预测，并基于预测误差更新模型。关键原则：

1. **层级生成模型**：从抽象概念到具体感觉的多层级预测
2. **预测误差最小化**：大脑通过以下方式最小化预测误差：
   - **知觉推理**：更新模型以适应感官数据
   - **主动推理**：作用于世界使感官数据符合预测
3. **自由能原理**：生物系统通过最小化变分自由能（惊讶的上界）抵抗无序
4. **精度加权**：注意调节分配给预测误差的精度（可靠性）

**情绪应用**：
- 情绪作为关于身体状态的预测（内感受预测）
- 焦虑作为对威胁相关预测误差的精度增强
- 抑郁作为关于自我效能和奖励的先验改变
- 情绪调节作为精度调节或先验更新

### 2. Narrative Identity & Life Story Model | 叙事身份与生命故事模型

**Source | 来源**: McAdams (2001, 2018, 2023); SEP Narrative Psychology (2024); Adler et al. (2016)

**English:**

Narrative identity is the internalized and evolving life story that integrates reconstructed past, perceived present, and anticipated future into a coherent whole with temporal depth, causal coherence, and thematic unity.

**Key Components**:

| Component | Description | Assessment |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| **Nuclear Episodes** | Specific memories that define self | High/low point memories, turning points |
| **Narrative Tone** | Overall emotional valence | Redemption vs. contamination sequences |
| **Motivational Themes** | Agency & communion strivings | Power/intimacy coding |
| **Ideological Setting** | Values, beliefs, meaning system | Value hierarchy, religious/spiritual orientation |
| **Generativity** | Concern for future generations | Generativity scale, legacy concerns |

**Narrative Coherence Dimensions**:
- **Temporal coherence**: Chronological ordering, causal connections
- **Thematic coherence**: Consistent themes across episodes
- **Autobiographical reasoning**: Making meaning from past events
- **Redemptive sequences**: Negative → positive transformations
- **Contaminative sequences**: Positive → negative transformations

**Well-Being Correlates**:
- Higher narrative coherence → better psychological well-being
- Redemption sequences → higher life satisfaction
- Contamination sequences → depression risk

**中文:**

叙事身份是内化的、 evolving 的生命故事，将重构的过去、感知的现在和预期的未来整合成一个具有时间深度、因果连贯性和主题一致性的连贯整体。

**关键组件**：

| 组件 | 描述 | 评估 |
|------|------|------|
| **核心情节** | 定义自我的特定记忆 | 高峰/低谷记忆、转折点 |
| **叙事基调** | 整体情绪效价 | 救赎 vs. 污染序列 |
| **动机主题** | 能动性与共融追求 | 权力/亲密编码 |
| **意识形态背景** | 价值观、信念、意义系统 | 价值层级、宗教/精神取向 |
| **繁衍性** | 对后代的关怀 | 繁衍性量表、遗产关注 |

**叙事连贯性维度**：
- **时间连贯性**：时间顺序、因果连接
- **主题连贯性**：跨情节的一致主题
- **自传体推理**：从过去事件中创造意义
- **救赎序列**：负面→正面转化
- **污染序列**：正面→负面转化

**幸福感相关性**：
- 更高叙事连贯性 → 更好心理健康
- 救赎序列 → 更高生活满意度
- 污染序列 → 抑郁风险

### 3. Moral Emotion & Social Norm Internalization | 道德情绪与社会规范内化

**Source | 来源**: SEP Moral Psychology (2024); Haidt (2012); Tangney et al. (2007); SEP Emotion & Ethics (2023)

**English:**

Moral emotions are emotions that are linked to the interests or welfare of society or groups and play a key role in moral judgment and behavior.

**Moral Emotion Taxonomy**:

| Category | Emotions | Function |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Other-condemning** | Anger, disgust, contempt | Enforce norms, punish violators |
| **Self-conscious** | Shame, guilt, embarrassment | Self-regulation, norm compliance |
| **Other-suffering** | Empathy, sympathy, compassion | Prosocial motivation, helping |
| **Other-praising** | Gratitude, elevation, admiration | Reinforce virtuous behavior |

**Guilt vs. Shame Distinction**:

| Feature | Guilt | Shame |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **Focus** | Specific behavior | Global self |
| **Appraisal** | "I did something bad" | "I am bad" |
| **Action tendency** | Repair, apologize | Hide, withdraw |
| **Outcome** | Adaptive (reparation) | Maladaptive (withdrawal) |
| **Social function** | Relationship repair | Social avoidance |

**Social Norm Internalization**:
- **External regulation**: Compliance to avoid punishment
- **Introjected regulation**: Partial internalization (guilt avoidance)
- **Identified regulation**: Personal endorsement of value
- **Integrated regulation**: Full integration with self-concept

**Moral Foundations (Haidt)**:
1. Care/Harm
2. Fairness/Cheating
3. Loyalty/Betrayal
4. Authority/Subversion
5. Sanctity/Degradation
6. Liberty/Oppression

**中文:**

道德情绪是与社会或群体利益或福祉相关的情绪，在道德判断和行为中起关键作用。

**道德情绪分类**：

| 类别 | 情绪 | 功能 |
|------|------|------|
| **他人谴责** | 愤怒、厌恶、蔑视 | 执行规范、惩罚违规者 |
| **自我意识** | 羞耻、内疚、尴尬 | 自我调节、规范遵守 |
| **他人痛苦** | 共情、同情、怜悯 | 亲社会动机、帮助 |
| **他人赞扬** | 感激、提升、钦佩 | 强化美德行为 |

**内疚 vs. 羞耻区分**：

| 特征 | 内疚 | 羞耻 |
|------|------|------|
| **焦点** | 特定行为 | 整体自我 |
| **评价** | "我做了坏事" | "我是坏人" |
| **行动倾向** | 修复、道歉 | 隐藏、退缩 |
| **结果** | 适应性（修复） | 适应不良（退缩） |
| **社会功能** | 关系修复 | 社会回避 |

**社会规范内化**：
- **外部调节**：为避免惩罚而遵守
- **内摄调节**：部分内化（避免内疚）
- **认同调节**：个人认可价值
- **整合调节**：与自我概念完全整合

**道德基础（Haidt）**：
1. 关爱/伤害
2. 公平/欺骗
3. 忠诚/背叛
4. 权威/颠覆
5. 圣洁/堕落
6. 自由/压迫
