**Irreducibility Claim Analysis | 不可还原性主张分析:**

```
Irreducibility Thesis (SEP §1):
├── Distribution of individual intentions ≠ collective intention
├── Common knowledge + mutual belief ≠ collective intentionality
├── Collective intention is primitive (Searle)
└── Individual Ownership Thesis must be reconciled with irreducibility

├── Collective intention attributed to individuals COLLECTIVELY
├── Not distributively (not sum of individual intentions)
├── No group mind (individual brains not integrated)
└── Mode account: same intention, different mode (we-mode vs. I-mode)
```
