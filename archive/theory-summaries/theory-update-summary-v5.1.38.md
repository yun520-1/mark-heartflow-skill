## Theoretical Foundations | 理论基础

### Cross-Cultural Well-Being | 跨文化幸福感

**English:**

Cross-cultural well-being research examines how cultural contexts shape the experience and expression of well-being:

**Individualism-Collectivism (Hofstede 1980, Triandis 1995)**:
- Individualist cultures: Well-being emphasizes personal achievement, autonomy, self-expression
- Collectivist cultures: Well-being emphasizes social harmony, family cohesion, group belonging
- PERMA adaptation: Relationships (R) weighted higher in collectivist cultures; Accomplishment (A) weighted higher in individualist cultures

**Independent-Interdependent Self (Markus & Kitayama 1991)**:
- Independent self (Western): Self as autonomous, unique, stable across contexts
- Interdependent self (Eastern): Self as relational, flexible, context-dependent
- Well-being implication: Self-esteem vs. relational harmony as well-being indicators

**Cultural Display Rules (Ekman 1972, Matsumoto 1990)**:
- Culturally-specific norms for emotional expression
- Display rules affect emotion regulation strategies and well-being outcomes
- Integration: Emotion prototype structure now includes cultural modulation layer

**Indigenous Well-Being Concepts**:
- Latin America: *Buen Vivir* (living well together)
- Africa: *Ubuntu* (I am because we are)
- East Asia: *Confucian harmony*, *Buddhist non-attachment*
- Integration: Expanded well-being ontology beyond Western frameworks

**中文:**

跨文化幸福感研究考察文化情境如何塑造幸福感的体验和表达：

**个人主义 - 集体主义（Hofstede 1980, Triandis 1995）**：
- 个人主义文化：幸福感强调个人成就、自主性、自我表达
- 集体主义文化：幸福感强调社会和谐、家庭凝聚、群体归属
- PERMA 适应：关系（R）在集体主义文化中权重更高；成就（A）在个人主义文化中权重更高

**独立 - 互依自我（Markus & Kitayama 1991）**：
- 独立自我（西方）：自我作为自主的、独特的、跨情境稳定的
- 互依自我（东方）：自我作为关系性的、灵活的、依赖情境的
- 幸福感含义：自尊 vs. 关系和谐作为幸福感指标

**文化表达规则（Ekman 1972, Matsumoto 1990）**：
- 文化特定的情绪表达规范
- 表达规则影响情绪调节策略和幸福感结果
- 整合：情绪原型结构现在包括文化调节层

**本土幸福感概念**：
- 拉丁美洲：*Buen Vivir*（共同美好生活）
- 非洲：*Ubuntu*（我在因我们在）
- 东亚：*儒家和谐*、*佛教无执*
- 整合：扩展幸福感本体论超越西方框架

### Digital Well-Being | 数字幸福感

**English:**

Digital well-being research examines the relationship between technology use and psychological flourishing:

**Technology Use Patterns (Przybylski 2017, Orben 2019)**:
- Goldilocks hypothesis: Moderate use optimal, both excessive and minimal use suboptimal
- Active vs. passive use: Active engagement (creating, connecting) enhances well-being; passive scrolling diminishes it
- Integration: Technology use assessment now distinguishes active/passive patterns

**Problematic Internet Use (Davis 2001, Caplan 2010)**:
- Cognitive preoccupation with internet
- Mood modification through internet use
- Tolerance, withdrawal, conflict, relapse patterns
- Integration: Screening protocols for problematic use integrated with clinical modules

**Social Media & Well-Being (Verduyn 2017, Hunt 2018)**:
- Social comparison processes (upward comparison → envy → reduced well-being)
- Fear of Missing Out (FoMO) as mediator
- Active use (direct interaction) vs. passive use (browsing) differential effects
- Integration: Social media use module connected to emotion regulation and self-esteem

**Digital Detox & Mindful Use (Radtke 2021, Newport 2019)**:
- Digital minimalism: Intentional technology use aligned with values
- Digital detox protocols: Structured breaks from technology
- Mindful technology use: Present-moment awareness during tech interaction
- Integration: Intervention protocols for healthy tech relationship

**中文:**

数字幸福感研究考察技术使用与心理繁荣之间的关系：

**技术使用模式（Przybylski 2017, Orben 2019）**：
- 金发姑娘假说：适度使用最优，过度使用和极少使用都次优
- 主动 vs. 被动使用：主动参与（创造、连接）增强幸福感；被动浏览降低幸福感
- 整合：技术使用评估现在区分主动/被动模式

**问题性网络使用（Davis 2001, Caplan 2010）**：
- 对网络的认知先占
- 通过网络使用进行情绪调节
- 耐受性、戒断、冲突、复发模式
- 整合：问题使用筛查协议与临床模块整合

**社交媒体与幸福感（Verduyn 2017, Hunt 2018）**：
- 社会比较过程（向上比较 → 嫉妒 → 幸福感降低）
- 错失恐惧（FoMO）作为中介
- 主动使用（直接互动）vs. 被动使用（浏览）的差异效应
- 整合：社交媒体使用模块与情绪调节和自尊连接

**数字排毒与正念使用（Radtke 2021, Newport 2019）**：
- 数字极简主义：与价值观一致的意向性技术使用
- 数字排毒协议：结构化技术中断
- 正念技术使用：技术互动期间的当下觉察
- 整合：健康技术关系的干预协议

### Environmental Psychology | 环境心理学

**English:**

Environmental psychology examines how physical environments affect psychological well-being:

**Biophilia Hypothesis (Wilson 1984)**:
- Innate human tendency to seek connections with nature
- Evolutionary basis: Nature exposure signaled safety, resources
- Integration: Nature connection as fundamental well-being need

**Nature Connectedness (Mayer & Frantz 2004)**:
- Cognitive and affective connection to natural world
- Measured by Nature Relatedness Scale (NRS)
- Higher connectedness → higher well-being, pro-environmental behavior
- Integration: Nature connectedness assessment and enhancement interventions

**Attention Restoration Theory (Kaplan 1995)**:
- Directed attention fatigue from urban/cognitive demands
- Natural environments provide "soft fascination" allowing restoration
- Four components: Being away, extent, fascination, compatibility
- Integration: Nature-based attention restoration protocols

**Green & Blue Space (Bratman 2019, White 2010)**:
- Green space: Parks, forests, gardens → stress reduction, mood improvement
- Blue space: Water bodies (ocean, lakes, rivers) → additional calming effects
- Integration: Environmental intervention recommendations

**Environmental Awe (Piff 2015)**:
- Awe experiences in natural settings (grand vistas, old-growth forests)
- Awe → small self → prosocial behavior, well-being
- Integration: Connected to aesthetic emotion module

**中文:**

环境心理学考察物理环境如何影响心理幸福感：

**亲生命假说（Wilson 1984）**：
- 人类寻求与自然连接的先天倾向
- 进化基础：自然暴露预示安全、资源
- 整合：自然连接作为基本幸福感需求

**自然连接度（Mayer & Frantz 2004）**：
- 与自然世界的认知和情感连接
- 通过自然连接量表（NRS）测量
- 更高连接度 → 更高幸福感、亲环境行为
- 整合：自然连接度评估和增强干预

**注意力恢复理论（Kaplan 1995）**：
- 城市/认知需求导致的定向注意力疲劳
- 自然环境提供"柔和魅力"允许恢复
- 四个组件：远离、范围、魅力、兼容
- 整合：基于自然的注意力恢复协议

**绿色与蓝色空间（Bratman 2019, White 2010）**：
- 绿色空间：公园、森林、花园 → 压力减轻、情绪改善
- 蓝色空间：水体（海洋、湖泊、河流）→ 额外镇静效应
- 整合：环境干预推荐

**环境敬畏（Piff 2015）**：
- 自然环境中的敬畏体验（壮丽景色、原始森林）
- 敬畏 → 小自我 → 亲社会行为、幸福感
- 整合：与审美情绪模块连接

### Sleep & Well-Being | 睡眠与幸福感

**English:**

Sleep research demonstrates bidirectional relationships between sleep quality and mental health:

**Sleep Quality Assessment (Buysse 1989)**:
- Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI): 7 components (quality, latency, duration, efficiency, disturbances, medication, daytime dysfunction)
- Integration: Sleep assessment protocol in well-being evaluation

**Circadian Rhythm Optimization (Roenneberg 2012)**:
- Chronotype assessment (morningness-eveningness)
- Social jetlag: Misalignment between biological and social time
- Light exposure timing for circadian entrainment
- Integration: Personalized sleep-wake scheduling

**Sleep Hygiene Protocols (Morin 2006)**:
- Stimulus control: Bed only for sleep/sex
- Sleep restriction: Limit time in bed to actual sleep time
- Relaxation training, cognitive techniques
- Integration: CBT-I (Insomnia) protocols connected to clinical modules

**Sleep-Emotion Connection (Walker 2017)**:
- Sleep deprivation → amygdala hyperactivity → emotional reactivity
- REM sleep for emotional memory processing
- Sleep quality affects emotion regulation capacity
- Integration: Sleep module connected to emotion regulation

**中文:**

睡眠研究证明睡眠质量与心理健康之间的双向关系：

**睡眠质量评估（Buysse 1989）**：
- 匹兹堡睡眠质量指数（PSQI）：7 个成分（质量、潜伏期、时长、效率、干扰、药物、日间功能障碍）
- 整合：幸福感评估中的睡眠评估协议

**昼夜节律优化（Roenneberg 2012）**：
- 时间类型评估（晨型 - 夜型）
- 社会时差：生物时间与社会时间错位
- 光照时间用于昼夜节律夹带
- 整合：个性化睡眠 - 觉醒调度

**睡眠卫生协议（Morin 2006）**：
- 刺激控制：床仅用于睡眠/性
- 睡眠限制：限制卧床时间至实际睡眠时间
- 放松训练、认知技术
- 整合：CBT-I（失眠）协议与临床模块连接

**睡眠 - 情绪连接（Walker 2017）**：
- 睡眠剥夺 → 杏仁核过度活跃 → 情绪反应性
- REM 睡眠用于情绪记忆加工
- 睡眠质量影响情绪调节能力
- 整合：睡眠模块与情绪调节连接

### Nutrition & Mental Health | 营养与心理健康

**English:**

Nutritional psychiatry examines diet-mental health connections:

**Gut-Brain Axis (Cryan & Dinan 2012)**:
- Bidirectional communication between gut microbiota and brain
- Microbiota produce neurotransmitters (serotonin, GABA, dopamine)
- Gut inflammation affects mood and cognition
- Integration: Gut health assessment in holistic well-being

**Mediterranean Diet & Mood (Sánchez-Villegas 2009)**:
- High in vegetables, fruits, whole grains, fish, olive oil
- Associated with reduced depression risk
- Anti-inflammatory, antioxidant mechanisms
- Integration: Dietary pattern recommendations

**Omega-3 & Depression (Grosso 2014)**:
- EPA and DHA critical for brain function
- Low omega-3 associated with depression
- Supplementation shows modest antidepressant effects
- Integration: Nutritional intervention protocols

**Micronutrients & Mental Health (Kaplan 2015)**:
- B vitamins, zinc, magnesium, iron critical for neurotransmitter synthesis
- Deficiencies linked to depression, anxiety
- Integration: Nutrient screening and supplementation guidance

营养精神病学考察饮食 - 心理健康连接：

- 整合：饮食模式推荐

**Omega-3 与抑郁（Grosso 2014）**：
- EPA 和 DHA 对大脑功能至关重要

- 补充显示适度抗抑郁效果
