## Key Theoretical Insights | 关键理论洞见

### 1. Embodied Emotion Generation | 具身情绪生成

**English:**
Emotions emerge from body state feedback, sensorimotor engagement, affordance actualization, predictive body regulation, and action readiness patterns.

**中文:**
情绪涌现于身体状态反馈、感觉运动参与、可供性实现、预测性身体调节和行动准备模式。

### 2. Active Inference Agency | 主动推理性能动性

**English:**
Agency emerges from minimizing expected free energy, selecting policies that fulfill predictions, balancing epistemic and pragmatic value, maintaining Markov blanket integrity, and sense of control through prediction success.

**中文:**
能动性涌现于最小化期望自由能、选择实现预测的政策、平衡认知性与实用性价值、维持马尔可夫毯完整性、通过预测成功获得控制感。

### 3. Direct Perception in Emotion | 情绪中的直接感知

**English:**
Emotional perception can be direct: affordances have emotional valence, threat/opportunity detection without inference, immediate emotional resonance, environmental emotional scaffolding, and social affordance perception.

**中文:**
情绪感知可以是直接的：可供性具有情绪效价、无需推理的威胁/机会检测、直接情绪共鸣、环境情绪支架、社会可供性感知。
