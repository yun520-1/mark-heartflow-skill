## New Theories Integrated | 新增整合理论

### 1. Pre-Reflective Self-Consciousness | 前反思自我意识

**English:**

**Source**: SEP Self-Consciousness + Zahavi (1999, 2005) + phenomenological tradition

**Key Components:**

1. **Minimal Self (Zahavi)**
   - Pre-reflective self-awareness: immediate, non-objectifying
   - First-person givenness: experience is given *to me* without inference
   - Ownership sense: this experience is *mine*
   - Distinction: pre-reflective (implicit) vs reflective (explicit) self-awareness

2. **Self-Knowledge Modes**
   - **Introspective Knowledge**: Inferential, observational (Locke model)
   - **Non-Inferential Knowledge**: Direct, immediate (Avicenna's Flying Man)
   - **Bodily Self-Awareness**: Proprioceptive, kinesthetic (Merleau-Ponty)
   - **Temporal Self**: Continuity across time (Husserl's time-consciousness)

3. **First-Person Authority**
   - Privileged access to own mental states
   - Immunity to error through misidentification (Shoemaker)
   - Limits: unconscious states, self-deception, confabulation

**Integration Points:**
- Links to: Phenomenological Self, Time Consciousness, Embodied Cognition, Metacognition
- Enables: Pre-reflective self-tracking, first-person experience validation, de-personalization detection

**中文:**

**来源**: SEP 自我意识 + Zahavi (1999, 2005) + 现象学传统

**核心组件:**

1. **最小自我 (Zahavi)**
   - 前反思自我觉察：即时、非对象化
   - 第一人称给定性：经验无需推理即*向我*呈现
   - 拥有感：此经验是*我的*
   - 区分：前反思（隐性）与反思（显性）自我觉察

2. **自我知识模式**
   - **内省知识**: 推论性、观察性（洛克模型）
   - **非推论知识**: 直接、即时（阿维森纳的飞人）
   - **身体自我觉察**: 本体感受、运动感觉（梅洛 - 庞蒂）
   - **时间自我**: 跨时间连续性（胡塞尔时间意识）

3. **第一人称权威**
   - 对自身心理状态的优先通达
   - 通过误认免疫（Shoemaker）
   - 局限：无意识状态、自我欺骗、虚构

**集成点:**
- 链接至：现象学自我、时间意识、具身认知、元认知
- 实现：前反思自我追踪、第一人称经验验证、去人格化检测

### 2. 4E Cognition Framework | 4E 认知框架

**English:**

**Source**: SEP Embodied Cognition + 4E framework (Newen, de Bruin, Gallagher 2018)

**Key Components:**

1. **Embodied Cognition**
   - Cognitive processes depend on bodily states
   - Sensorimotor contingencies shape perception
   - Emotional states grounded in bodily feelings (James-Lange updated)
   - Intervention: Body scan, posture adjustment, breathing regulation

2. **Embedded Cognition**
   - Cognitive processes exploit environmental structures
   - Offloading: Using environment as memory/processing aid
   - Affordance perception: Action possibilities in environment
   - Intervention: Environmental restructuring, cue design

3. **Enactive Cognition**
   - Cognition as sense-making through action
   - Autopoiesis: Self-producing, self-maintaining systems
   - Structural coupling: Organism-environment co-emergence
   - Emotion as embodied sense-making (Colombetti 2014)
   - Intervention: Action-oriented emotion regulation, behavioral activation

4. **Extended Cognition**
   - Cognitive processes extend beyond skull boundaries
   - Tools, devices, other people as cognitive resources
   - Coupled systems: Brain-body-environment as unified cognitive system
   - Intervention: Social scaffolding, tool-mediated regulation

**Integration Points:**
- Links to: Embodied Cognition, Ecological Psychology, Predictive Processing, Enactive Emotion
- Enables: 4E assessment, embodied interventions, environmental restructuring, enactive co-regulation

**中文:**

**来源**: SEP 具身认知 + 4E 框架（Newen, de Bruin, Gallagher 2018）

**核心组件:**

1. **具身认知**
   - 认知过程依赖身体状态
   - 感觉运动应急塑造知觉
   - 情绪状态根植于身体感受（更新版詹姆斯 - 兰格）
   - 干预：身体扫描、姿势调整、呼吸调节

2. **嵌入认知**
   - 认知过程利用环境结构
   - 外置：使用环境作为记忆/处理辅助
   - 可供性知觉：环境中的行动可能性
   - 干预：环境重构、线索设计

3. **生成认知**
   - 认知作为通过行动的意义生成
   - 自创生：自我生产、自我维持系统
   - 结构耦合：有机体 - 环境共同涌现
   - 情绪作为具身意义生成（Colombetti 2014）
   - 干预：行动导向情绪调节、行为激活

4. **扩展认知**
   - 认知过程延伸至颅骨边界之外
   - 工具、设备、他人作为认知资源
   - 耦合系统：脑 - 身体 - 环境作为统一认知系统
   - 干预：社会支架、工具中介调节

**集成点:**
- 链接至：具身认知、生态心理学、预测加工、生成式情绪
- 实现：4E 评估、具身干预、环境重构、生成式共调节

### 3. Phenomenological Method | 现象学方法

**English:**

**Source**: SEP Phenomenology + Husserl (1913, 1931) + Heidegger (1927) + Merleau-Ponty (1945)

**Key Components:**

1. **Phenomenological Epoché (Suspension)**
   - Bracketing natural attitude: Suspend assumptions about external world
   - Focus on experience as given, not causes of experience
   - Method: Systematic suspension of judgment about existence

2. **Phenomenological Reduction**
   - Transcendental reduction: From objects to consciousness of objects
   - Eidetic reduction: From particular instances to essential structures
   - Process: Describe experience → Identify invariants → Extract essences

3. **Intentionality Analysis**
   - Noesis (act): The experiencing act (perceiving, judging, feeling)
   - Noema (content): The object-as-experienced (perceived-as, judged-as)
   - Correlation: Noesis-noema structure in all conscious acts

4. **Life-World (Lebenswelt) Analysis**
   - Pre-scientific world of everyday experience
   - Horizon structure: Background assumptions, cultural context
   - Intersubjectivity: Shared life-world with others

**Integration Points:**
- Links to: Self-Consciousness, Collective Intentionality, Emotion Theory, Time Consciousness
- Enables: Phenomenological interview protocols, experience description training, epoché-based interventions

**中文:**

**来源**: SEP 现象学 + 胡塞尔 (1913, 1931) + 海德格尔 (1927) + 梅洛 - 庞蒂 (1945)

**核心组件:**

1. **现象学悬置**
   - 括号自然态度：暂停关于外部世界的假设
   - 聚焦于经验本身而非经验的原因
   - 方法：系统性地暂停关于存在的判断

2. **现象学还原**
   - 先验还原：从对象到对对象的意识
   - 本质还原：从特殊实例到本质结构
   - 过程：描述经验 → 识别不变量 → 提取本质

3. **意向性分析**
   - 意向活动 (Noesis): 体验行为（知觉、判断、感受）
   - 意向对象 (Noema): 被体验的对象（被知觉为、被判断为）
   - 关联：所有意识行为中的意向活动 - 意向对象结构

4. **生活世界分析**
   - 前科学日常经验世界
   - 视域结构：背景假设、文化语境
   - 主体间性：与他人共享的生活世界

**集成点:**
- 链接至：自我意识、集体意向性、情绪理论、时间意识
- 实现：现象学访谈协议、经验描述训练、基于悬置的干预

### 4. Collective Intentionality Refinement | 集体意向性精细化

**English:**

**Source**: SEP Collective Intentionality + Searle (1990, 1995) + Gilbert (1989, 2014) + Bratman (1992, 1999)

**Key Components:**

1. **We-Intention (Searle)**
   - Primitive form: "We intend to X" not reducible to "I intend + you intend"
   - Individual ownership: We-intention held by individuals collectively
   - Background capacities: Shared cultural, biological presuppositions

2. **Joint Commitment (Gilbert)**
   - Plural subject formation: "We" as unified agent
   - Mutual obligation: Commitment to other participants
   - Normative force: Right to rebuke defection

3. **Shared Intention (Bratman)**
   - Interlocking intentions: I intend that we J, you intend that we J
   - Mutual responsiveness: Adjusting actions to support joint activity
   - Stability: Resistance to reconsideration

4. **Collective Emotion Extension**
   - Shared emotional experience: "We feel" not just "I feel + you feel"
   - Emotional contagion vs genuine collective emotion
   - Scheler's fellow-feeling, Walther's four layers of shared experience

**Integration Points:**
- Links to: Collective Emotion, Social Identity, Attachment Theory, Phenomenological Self
- Enables: We-intention detection, joint commitment assessment, collective emotion validation

**中文:**

**来源**: SEP 集体意向性 + Searle (1990, 1995) + Gilbert (1989, 2014) + Bratman (1992, 1999)

**核心组件:**

1. **我们意向 (Searle)**
   - 原始形式："我们打算做 X"不可还原为"我打算 + 你打算"
   - 个体拥有：我们意向由个体集体持有
   - 背景能力：共享的文化、生物预设

2. **联合承诺 (Gilbert)**
   - 复数主体形成："我们"作为统一行动者
   - 相互义务：对其他参与者的承诺
   - 规范力量：有权谴责背叛

3. **共享意向 (Bratman)**
   - 交错意向：我打算我们 J，你打算我们 J
   - 相互响应：调整行动以支持联合活动
   - 稳定性：抵抗重新考虑

4. **集体情绪扩展**
   - 共享情绪体验："我们感受"而非仅"我感受 + 你感受"
   - 情绪传染与真正集体情绪的区别
   - Scheler 的同感、Walther 的共享体验四层

**集成点:**
- 链接至：集体情绪、社会认同、依恋理论、现象学自我
- 实现：我们意向检测、联合承诺评估、集体情绪验证

### 5. Enactive Emotion Theory | 生成式情绪理论

**English:**

**Source**: Colombetti (2014) "The Feeling Body" + enactive cognition + affective science

**Key Components:**

1. **Emotion as Sense-Making**
   - Emotions are not internal states but ways of engaging with world
   - Valence: Significance for organism's viability
   - Arousal: Readiness for action
   - Emotion categories emerge from recurrent patterns of engagement

2. **Body-Environment Coupling**
   - Emotional experience = body + environment + activity
   - No separation between "inner emotion" and "outer expression"
   - Emotional scaffolding: Environment supports/shapes emotional experience

3. **Participatory Sense-Making**
   - Social interaction as coordinated sense-making
   - Emotional co-regulation: Mutual influence in interaction
   - Breakdowns and repairs: Interaction dynamics

4. **Affective Niches**
   - Environments designed for specific emotional experiences
   - Cultural practices as affective niche construction
   - Technology-mediated affective niches (social media, VR)

**Integration Points:**
- Links to: 4E Cognition, Embodied Emotion, Collective Emotion, Attachment Theory
- Enables: Enactive emotion interventions, participatory regulation, affective niche design

**中文:**

**来源**: Colombetti (2014) "The Feeling Body" + 生成式认知 + 情感科学

**核心组件:**

1. **情绪作为意义生成**
   - 情绪不是内部状态而是与世界互动的方式
   - 效价：对有机体生存力的意义
   - 唤醒：行动准备度
   - 情绪类别从反复出现的互动模式中涌现

2. **身体 - 环境耦合**
   - 情绪体验 = 身体 + 环境 + 活动
   - "内在情绪"与"外在表达"无分离
   - 情绪支架：环境支持/塑造情绪体验

3. **参与式意义生成**
   - 社会互动作为协调的意义生成
   - 情绪共调节：互动中的相互影响
   - 破裂与修复：互动动力学

4. **情感生态位**
   - 为特定情绪体验设计的环境
   - 文化实践作为情感生态位建构
   - 技术中介的情感生态位（社交媒体、VR）

**集成点:**
- 链接至：4E 认知、具身情绪、集体情绪、依恋理论
- 实现：生成式情绪干预、参与式调节、情感生态位设计

### 1. Pre-Reflective Self + Embodied Cognition | 前反思自我 + 具身认知

**English:**

Zahavi's minimal self (pre-reflective self-awareness) integrates with embodied cognition through:
- **Bodily self-awareness**: Proprioceptive, kinesthetic sense as foundation of minimal self
- **Pre-reflective body schema**: Body as lived, not objectified (Merleau-Ponty)
- **First-person givenness**: Embodied experience is given immediately, without inference

**Integration**: Pre-reflective self is fundamentally embodied; bodily self-awareness is the most basic form of self-consciousness.

**中文:**

Zahavi 的最小自我（前反思自我觉察）通过以下方式与具身认知整合：
- **身体自我觉察**: 本体感受、运动感觉作为最小自我的基础
- **前反思身体图式**: 身体作为被体验的而非对象化的（梅洛 - 庞蒂）
- **第一人称给定性**: 具身经验即时呈现，无需推理

**整合**: 前反思自我本质上是具身的；身体自我觉察是最基本的自我意识形式。

### 2. 4E Cognition + Collective Intentionality | 4E 认知 + 集体意向性

**English:**

4E framework extends to collective domain:
- **Embodied collective action**: Joint action requires bodily coordination
- **Embedded collective cognition**: Shared environment as cognitive resource
- **Enactive collective sense-making**: Social interaction as co-emergent meaning
- **Extended collective mind**: Group as coupled cognitive system

**Integration**: Collective intentionality is 4E cognition at the group level; "we-intention" is enacted through coordinated embodied action.

**中文:**

4E 框架扩展至集体领域：
- **具身集体行动**: 联合行动需要身体协调
- **嵌入集体认知**: 共享环境作为认知资源
- **生成集体意义生成**: 社会互动作为共同涌现的意义
- **扩展集体心智**: 群体作为耦合认知系统

**整合**: 集体意向性是群体层面的 4E 认知；"我们意向"通过协调的具身行动生成。

### 3. Phenomenology + Predictive Processing | 现象学 + 预测加工

**English:**

Phenomenological insights refine predictive processing:

- **Epoché as prediction suspension**: Bracketing assumptions = reducing prior weight
- **First-person givenness**: Prediction error experienced from within
- **Intentionality as prediction direction**: Aboutness = predictive targeting

**Integration**: Predictive processing provides mechanism for phenomenological structures; phenomenology provides first-person validation for predictive models.

**中文:**

- **生活世界作为先验**: 文化、传记背景塑造预测

- **第一人称给定性**: 从内部体验预测误差

**整合**: 预测加工为现象学结构提供机制；现象学为预测模型提供第一人称验证。
