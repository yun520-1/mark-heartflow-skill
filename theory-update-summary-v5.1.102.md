**Solution**: Dual-layer model (Aquinas synthesis):
- Layer 1: Existence-awareness (mere presence suffices) ← Avicenna/Plato
- Layer 2: Essence-awareness (requires outer cognition) ← Aristotle

**中文:**

- 第 1 层：存在意识 (心灵在场即足) ← 阿维森纳/柏拉图
- 第 2 层：本质意识 (需外在认知) ← 亚里士多德
