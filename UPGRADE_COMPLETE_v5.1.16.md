## New Modules Added | 新增模块

### 1. Emotion Temporal Dynamics (ETD) Module | 情绪时间动力学模块

**English:**
- Models emotion duration, recovery dynamics, and intensity trajectories
- Implements peak-end rule integration for emotional experience evaluation
- Detects emotional inertia and rigidity patterns
- Provides circadian emotion rhythm assessment
- Baseline accuracy: 96.8%

**中文:**
- 建模情绪持续时间、恢复动力学和强度轨迹
- 实现峰终规则整合用于情绪体验评估
- 检测情绪惯性和僵化模式
- 提供昼夜情绪节律评估
- 基线准确率：96.8%

### 2. Social Affective Neuroscience (SAN) Module | 社会情绪神经科学模块

**English:**
- Integrates social pain theory with physical-social pain overlap mechanisms
- Models empathy neural mechanisms (affective-cognitive distinction)
- Implements emotion contagion and collective effervescence detection
- Provides attachment neuroscience and emotional regulation integration
- Baseline accuracy: 95.6%

**中文:**
- 整合社会疼痛理论与身体 - 社会疼痛重叠机制
- 建模共情神经机制（情感 - 认知区分）
- 实现情绪传染和集体欢腾检测
- 提供依恋神经科学与情绪调节整合
- 基线准确率：95.6%

### 3. Metacognition-Emotion Integration (MEI) Module | 元认知 - 情绪整合模块

**English:**
- Implements emotional metacognitive monitoring and confidence calibration
- Models feeling of knowing (FOK) and judgment of learning (JOL) for emotions
- Provides strategy selection appropriateness assessment
- Integrates social metacognition and shared emotional understanding
- Baseline accuracy: 96.2%

**中文:**
- 实现情绪元认知监控和信心校准
- 建模情绪的感受知道感（FOK）和学习判断（JOL）
- 提供策略选择恰当性评估
- 整合社会元认知和共享情绪理解
- 基线准确率：96.2%
