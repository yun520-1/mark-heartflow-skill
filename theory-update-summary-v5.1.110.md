**Key Theoretical Insights | 关键理论洞见:**

1. **Life Story Model of Identity (LSMI) | 生命故事身份模型**: Identity is the internalized and evolving life story that integrates the reconstructed past, perceived present, and anticipated future (McAdams 2001)
2. **Autobiographical Reasoning | 自传体推理**: The process of deriving meaning from past events to create coherence and continuity in the life story (Habermas & Bluck 2000)
3. **Redemption Sequences | 救赎序列**: Narrative pattern where bad/contaminating events are followed by good/redemptive outcomes (associated with well-being)
4. **Contamination Sequences | 污染序列**: Narrative pattern where good events are followed by bad outcomes (associated with depression)
5. **Narrative Coherence | 叙事连贯性**: The degree to which life stories exhibit thematic, causal, and temporal coherence

## Appendix: Narrative Psychology Assessment Protocol | 附录：叙事心理学评估协议

**English:**

### Life Story Interview (LSI) Protocol

1. **Life Chapters** | 生命章节
   - "Imagine your life as a book with chapters. Describe key chapters."

2. **Key Scenes** | 关键场景
   - High point, low point, turning point, earliest memory, important childhood, adolescent, adult scenes

3. **Life Themes** | 生命主题
   - "What is the central theme or message of your life story?"

4. **Future Script** | 未来脚本
   - "How do you see your life story unfolding in the future?"
