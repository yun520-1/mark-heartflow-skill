## Technical Details | 技术细节

### Phenomenal Concept Strategy v2.0 | 现象概念策略 v2.0

**English:**

The phenomenal concept strategy explains the epistemic gap between physical and phenomenal knowledge without positing an ontological gap. Key components:

1. **Recognitional Concepts**: Phenomenal concepts as direct recognitional abilities
2. **Indexical Analysis**: "This experience" as indexical reference to current experience
3. **Dual Concept Theory**: Physical and phenomenal concepts as different modes of presentation
4. **Epistemic-Ontological Distinction**: The gap is conceptual, not metaphysical

**中文:**

现象概念策略解释物理知识与现象知识之间的认识论鸿沟，而不设定本体论鸿沟。关键组件：

1. **识别概念**: 现象概念作为直接识别能力
2. **索引词分析**: "这个经验"作为对当前经验的索引词指称
3. **双重概念理论**: 物理和现象概念作为不同的呈现方式
4. **认识论 - 本体论区分**: 鸿沟是概念性的，非形而上学的

### Knowledge Argument Analysis v1.0 | 知识论证分析 v1.0

**English:**

Frank Jackson's Knowledge Argument challenges physicalism through Mary's Room thought experiment. Our analysis includes:

**Argument Structure:**
- P1: Mary knows all physical facts about color vision
- P2: Mary learns something new upon seeing red
- C: Therefore, not all facts are physical facts

**Five Physicalist Responses:**
1. Ability Hypothesis (knowing-how vs. knowing-that)
2. Phenomenal Concepts (new concepts, not new facts)
3. Representationalism (phenomenal character = representational content)
4. Illusionism (phenomenal properties as useful fictions)
5. A Priori Physicalism (conceptual competence limitation)

**中文:**

弗兰克·杰克逊的知识论证通过玛丽房间思想实验挑战物理主义。我们的分析包括：

**论证结构:**
- P1: 玛丽知道关于色觉的所有物理事实
- P2: 玛丽看见红色时学到新东西
- C: 因此，非所有事实都是物理事实

**五种物理主义回应:**
1. 能力假说（能力之知 vs. 事实之知）
2. 现象概念（新概念，非新事实）
3. 表征主义（现象特征=表征内容）
4. 幻觉主义（现象性质作为有用的虚构）
5. 先验物理主义（概念能力限制）
