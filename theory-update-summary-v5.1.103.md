## New Theoretical Integrations | 新理论整合

### 1. Temporal Consciousness Triad | 时间意识三元组

**English:**

Integrated three major models of temporal consciousness from SEP and phenomenological tradition:

| Model | Key Thinker | Core Concept | Integration |
|-------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Husserlian Triad** | Edmund Husserl | Protention-Primordial Impression-Retention | Temporal structure of experience |
| **Specious Present** | William James | "Saddle-back" with bow and stern | Perceived duration (~3-12 seconds) |
| **Being-Towards-Death** | Martin Heidegger | Temporality as being's horizon | Existential time depth |

**Husserl's Temporal Structure**:
- **Protention**: Anticipation of future (forward-looking)
- **Primordial Impression**: Immediate now-point
- **Retention**: Just-past held in consciousness (not memory)

**James's Specious Present**:
- Not a knife-edge moment
- Has duration with "bow" (future) and "stern" (past)
- Empirical range: 3-12 seconds for conscious integration

**Heideggerian Temporality**:
- Time as horizon of being
- Authentic temporality: finite, death-aware
- Inauthentic temporality: everyday clock-time

**中文:**

整合 SEP 和现象学传统的三大时间意识模型：

| 模型 | 关键思想家 | 核心概念 | 整合 |
|------|-----------|---------|------|
| **胡塞尔三元组** | Edmund Husserl | 前摄 - 原印象 - 滞留 | 体验的时间结构 |
| **显似现在** | William James | 带"船头船尾"的鞍背 | 感知持续时间 (约 3-12 秒) |
| **向死存在** | Martin Heidegger | 时间作为存在的视域 | 存在时间深度 |

**胡塞尔时间结构**:
- **前摄 (Protention)**: 对未来的预期 (向前看)
- **原印象 (Primordial Impression)**: 直接的现在点
- **滞留 (Retention)**: 刚过去保留在意识中 (非记忆)

**詹姆斯的显似现在**:
- 非刀刃式瞬间
- 具有持续时间的"船头"(未来) 和"船尾"(过去)
- 经验范围：意识整合约 3-12 秒

**海德格尔时间性**:
- 时间作为存在的视域
- 本真时间性：有限的、死亡意识的
- 非本真时间性：日常钟表时间

**Integration Status | 整合状态**: ✅ Complete

### 2. 4E Cognition Framework | 4E 认知框架

**English:**

Integrated the four E's of embodied cognition from SEP:

| E | Name | Description | Application |
|---|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Embodied** | 具身的 | Cognition depends on body properties | Body state scanning, sensorimotor coupling |
| **Embedded** | 嵌入的 | Cognition occurs in environmental context | Situational assessment, affordance detection |
| **Enactive** | 生成的 | Cognition through action-perception loops | Active inference, intervention generation |
| **Extended** | 延展的 | Cognition extends to tools/environment | External scaffolding, distributed cognition |

**Key Insights from SEP Embodied Cognition**:

1. **Anti-Cartesian**: Rejects mind-body dualism
2. **Phenomenological Grounding**: Merleau-Ponty's lived body
3. **Dynamical Systems**: Time-evolving body-environment coupling
4. **Ecological Psychology**: Gibson's affordances, no impoverished stimuli

**中文:**

整合 SEP 具身认知的四个 E：

| E | 名称 | 描述 | 应用 |
|---|------|------|------|

| **Enactive** | 生成的 | 通过行动 - 感知循环认知 | 主动推理、干预生成 |
| **Extended** | 延展的 | 认知延展至工具/环境 | 外部支架、分布式认知 |

**SEP 具身认知关键洞见**:
