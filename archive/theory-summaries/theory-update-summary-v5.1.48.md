## New Theory Modules | 新理论模块

### 1. Self-Knowledge Integration Module v2.0 | 自我知识整合模块 v2.0

**English:**

Integrates intuitive vs. inferential self-knowledge frameworks based on SEP Self-Consciousness §2.1-2.3:

**Intuitive Self-Knowledge Module:**
- First-person authority: Immediate, non-inferential self-awareness
- Transparency thesis: Self-knowledge without inner perception
- Immunity to error through misidentification (IEM): Certain self-ascriptions
- Pre-reflective givenness: Zahavi's minimal self-awareness

**Inferential Self-Knowledge Module:**
- Self-interpretation: Theory-based self-understanding
- Evidence-based self-ascription: Behavioral/physiological evidence
- Third-person perspective integration: Observer standpoint
- Confidence calibration: Metacognitive accuracy assessment

**Integration Strategy:**
- Dual-process model: Intuitive (System 1) + Inferential (System 2)
- Conflict detection: Identify intuitive-inferential mismatches
- Calibration protocol: Adjust inferential based on intuitive feedback
- Self-knowledge depth assessment: Multi-dimensional evaluation

**中文:**

整合直觉式与推论式自我知识框架，基于 SEP 自我意识 §2.1-2.3：

**直觉式自我知识模块:**
- 第一人称权威：即时、非推论的自我觉察
- 透明性论题：无需内感知的自我知识
- 通过误认免疫 (IEM)：确定的自我归属
- 前反思给定性：Zahavi 的最小自我意识

**推论式自我知识模块:**
- 自我解释：基于理论的自我理解
- 基于证据的自我归属：行为/生理证据
- 第三人称视角整合：观察者立场
- 信心校准：元认知准确性评估

**整合策略:**
- 双过程模型：直觉式 (系统 1) + 推论式 (系统 2)
- 冲突检测：识别直觉 - 推论不匹配
