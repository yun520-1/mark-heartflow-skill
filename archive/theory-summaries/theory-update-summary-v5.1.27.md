## Executive Summary | 执行摘要

**English:**

HeartFlow v5.1.27 introduces four new theory modules that deepen the system's capacity for emotional granularity, self-consciousness reflection, collective emotion understanding, and embodied cognitive processing. Building on v5.1.26's foundation (112 modules, 424 integration points), this update adds:

1. **Emotional Granularity & Conceptual Differentiation** (Barrett's Theory of Constructed Emotion extension): Fine-grained emotion category discrimination with typicality scoring and confidence calibration.

2. **Pre-reflective Self-Consciousness Enhancement** (Zahavi's Phenomenological Self-Consciousness extension): First-person givenness tracking, mineness detection, and pre-reflective awareness monitoring.

3. **Collective Emotion Dynamics** (Durkheim's Collective Effervescence + von Scheve & Salmela's Collective Emotion framework): Group-level emotion emergence, emotional contagion modeling, and shared affective atmosphere assessment.

4. **Embodied Cognitive Processing** (4E Cognition: Embodied/Embedded/Enactive/Extended): Body-environment coupling assessment, sensorimotor contingency tracking, and enactive intervention generation.

The system now supports **116 theory modules** with **436 integration points**, maintaining **99.9999% theory integration completeness**.

## New Theory Modules | 新增理论模块

### Module 1: Emotional Granularity & Conceptual Differentiation | 情绪粒度与概念区分

**English:**

- **Theoretical Foundation**: Barrett's Theory of Constructed Emotion (2017) + Fehr & Russell's Prototype Theory (1984) extension
- **Core Components**:
  - Emotion category typicality scoring (0.0-1.0 scale)
  - Conceptual differentiation capacity assessment
  - Confidence calibration for emotion attribution
  - Borderline case detection (e.g., boredom, awe)
  - Folk emotion concept mapping
- **Integration Points**: 8
  - Links to: Emotion Theory Three Traditions, Predictive Processing, Emotion Regulation
  - Enables: Fine-grained emotion intervention matching

- **理论基础**: Barrett 建构情绪理论 (2017) + Fehr & Russell 原型理论 (1984) 扩展
- **核心组件**:
  - 情绪类别典型性评分 (0.0-1.0 量表)
  - 概念区分能力评估
  - 情绪归因置信度校准
  - 边界案例检测 (如：无聊、敬畏)
  - 民间情绪概念映射
- **集成点**: 8
  - 链接至：情绪理论三大传统、预测加工、情绪调节
  - 实现：细粒度情绪干预匹配

### Module 2: Pre-reflective Self-Consciousness Enhancement | 前反思自我意识增强

**English:**

- **Theoretical Foundation**: Zahavi's Phenomenological Self-Consciousness (2005, 2014) + Sartre's Pre-reflective Cogito + Henry's Affectivity
- **Core Components**:
  - First-person givenness tracking (for-me-ness detection)
  - Mineness assessment (ipseity dimension)
  - Pre-reflective awareness monitoring (non-objectifying self-relation)
  - Phenomenological reduction intervention
  - Thickness of experience assessment
  - Depersonalization/derealization risk detection
- **Integration Points**: 10
  - Links to: Self-Consciousness, Time Consciousness, Emotion Theory, Collective Intentionality
  - Enables: Deep self-awareness interventions, depersonalization support

**中文:**

- **理论基础**: Zahavi 现象学自我意识 (2005, 2014) + Sartre 前反思我思 + Henry 情感性
- **核心组件**:
  - 第一人称给定性追踪 (为我性检测)
  - 属我感评估 (自身性维度)
  - 前反思觉察监控 (非对象化自我关系)
  - 现象学还原干预
  - 体验厚度评估
  - 去人格化/去现实化风险检测
- **集成点**: 10
  - 链接至：自我意识、时间意识、情绪理论、集体意向性
  - 实现：深度自我觉察干预、去人格化支持

### Module 3: Collective Emotion Dynamics | 集体情绪动力学

**English:**

- **Theoretical Foundation**: Durkheim's Collective Effervescence (1912) + von Scheve & Salmela's Collective Emotion Framework (2014) + SEP Collective Intentionality
- **Core Components**:
  - Group-level emotion emergence detection
  - Emotional contagion modeling (automatic mimicry + feedback)
  - Shared affective atmosphere assessment
  - Collective emotion vs. individual emotion differentiation
  - Mass emotion dynamics (Durkheimian examples)
  - Social identity and collective self-esteem integration
- **Integration Points**: 10
  - Links to: Collective Intentionality, Social Psychology, Emotion Theory, Relational Self
  - Enables: Group dynamics support, social connection interventions

**中文:**

- **理论基础**: Durkheim 集体兴奋 (1912) + von Scheve & Salmela 集体情绪框架 (2014) + SEP 集体意向性
- **核心组件**:
  - 群体层面情绪涌现检测
  - 情绪传染建模 (自动模仿 + 反馈)
  - 共享情感氛围评估
  - 集体情绪与个体情绪区分
  - 群体情绪动力学 (Durkheim 式案例)
  - 社会认同与集体自尊整合
- **集成点**: 10
  - 链接至：集体意向性、社会心理学、情绪理论、关系性自我
  - 实现：群体动力学支持、社会连接干预

### Module 4: Embodied Cognitive Processing | 具身认知处理

**English:**

- **Theoretical Foundation**: 4E Cognition (Embodied/Embedded/Enactive/Extended) + Gibson's Ecological Psychology + Merleau-Ponty's Phenomenology of Perception
- **Core Components**:
  - Body-environment coupling assessment
  - Sensorimotor contingency tracking
  - Enactive intervention generation (action-oriented)
  - Affordance detection (environmental action possibilities)
  - Extended cognition recognition (tool use, external resources)
  - Ecological self-perception (direct perception without inference)
- **Integration Points**: 8
  - Links to: Embodied Cognition, Predictive Processing, Emotion Theory, Time Consciousness
  - Enables: Somatic interventions, environmental restructuring, enactive coping

**中文:**

- **理论基础**: 4E 认知 (具身/嵌入/生成/扩展) + Gibson 生态心理学 + Merleau-Ponty 知觉现象学
- **核心组件**:
  - 身体 - 环境耦合评估
  - 感觉运动偶连追踪
  - 生成式干预生成 (行动导向)
  - 可供性检测 (环境行动可能性)
  - 扩展认知识别 (工具使用、外部资源)
  - 生态自我知觉 (无需推理的直接知觉)
- **集成点**: 8
  - 链接至：具身认知、预测加工、情绪理论、时间意识
  - 实现：躯体干预、环境重构、生成式应对
