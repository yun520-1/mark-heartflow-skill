### 1. Temporal Self-Integration Theory v1.0 | 时间自我整合理论 v1.0

**Source | 来源**: SEP Temporal Consciousness + SEP Self-Consciousness + Husserl (1891-1928) + William James (1890) + McAdams (2001, 2006) + Zimbardo (1999, 2008)

**English:**

Temporal Self-Integration Theory proposes that self-identity is fundamentally temporal, constituted by the dynamic integration of past, present, and future orientations. This module integrates:

- **Husserl's Triadic Temporal Structure**: Primal impression (now-point), retention (just-past), and protention (anticipated future) as the foundation of temporal consciousness.
- **William James' Specious Present**: The experienced "now" as a duration-block containing both immediate past and anticipated future.
- **Narrative Identity Theory (McAdams)**: Life stories as the psychological construction of self-continuity across time.
- **Time Perspective Theory (Zimbardo)**: Balanced time perspective as optimal psychological functioning across past-positive, past-negative, present-hedonistic, present-fatalistic, and future orientations.
- **Temporal Depth in Psychotherapy**: The capacity to experience and integrate multiple temporal layers simultaneously.

**中文:**

时间自我整合理论提出，自我认同本质上是时间性的，由过去、现在和未来取向的动态整合构成。本模块整合：

- **胡塞尔的三元时间结构**: 原印象 (现在点)、滞留 (刚过去) 和前摄 (预期未来) 作为时间意识的基础。
- **威廉·詹姆斯的显似现在**: 体验到的"现在"作为包含直接过去和预期未来的持续块。
- **叙事身份理论 (McAdams)**: 生命故事作为跨时间自我连续性的心理建构。
- **时间视角理论 (Zimbardo)**: 平衡时间视角作为在过去积极、过去消极、现在享乐、现在宿命和未来取向之间的最佳心理功能。
- **心理治疗中的时间深度**: 同时体验和整合多个时间层次的能力。

### 2. Narrative Identity Continuity v1.0 | 叙事身份连续性 v1.0

**Source | 来源**: McAdams (2001, 2006, 2011) + SEP Narrative Psychology + Habermas & Bluck (2000) + Adler (2012)

**English:**

Narrative Identity Continuity module provides comprehensive assessment and intervention tools for life story coherence, redemption/contamination sequences, and autobiographical reasoning. Key components:

- **Life Story Model of Identity**: Identity as an internalized, evolving story of the self that provides life with unity, purpose, and meaning.
- **Redemption Sequences**: Narrative patterns where bad turns to good, suffering leads to growth, or loss yields gain.
- **Contamination Sequences**: Narrative patterns where good turns to bad, joy is spoiled, or hopes are dashed.
- **Autobiographical Reasoning**: The cognitive process of drawing connections between life events and self-understanding.
- **Narrative Coherence Assessment**: Evaluating temporal, causal, and thematic coherence in life stories.
- **Life Story Interview (LSI) Protocol**: Structured assessment of key life scenes, characters, and themes.

**中文:**

叙事身份连续性模块提供生命故事连贯性、救赎/污染序列和自传体推理的全面评估和干预工具。关键组件：

- **身份的生命故事模型**: 身份作为内在化的、不断发展的自我故事，为生活提供统一性、目的和意义。
- **救赎序列**: 叙事模式，其中坏变好、痛苦导致成长、或损失产生收获。
- **污染序列**: 叙事模式，其中好变坏、快乐被破坏、或希望破灭。
- **自传体推理**: 在生活事件和自我理解之间建立联系的认知过程。
- **叙事连贯性评估**: 评估生命故事中的时间、因果和主题连贯性。
- **生命故事访谈 (LSI) 方案**: 对关键生活场景、人物和主题的结构化评估。

### 3. Time Perspective Therapy v1.0 | 时间视角治疗 v1.0

**Source | 来源**: Zimbardo & Boyd (1999, 2008) + Sword & Sword (2012) + Balanced Time Perspective Research (2014-2025)

**English:**

Time Perspective Therapy module provides assessment and intervention for developing balanced time perspective across six dimensions:

- **Past-Positive**: Warm, nostalgic orientation toward the past
- **Past-Negative**: Pessimistic, regretful orientation toward the past
- **Present-Hedonistic**: Pleasure-seeking, risk-taking present orientation
- **Present-Fatalistic**: Helpless, hopeless present orientation
- **Future**: Goal-oriented, planning-focused orientation
- **Future-Transcendent**: Orientation toward afterlife or long-term legacy (added 2014)

**Balanced Time Perspective Profile**:
- High Past-Positive (moderate)
- Low Past-Negative
- Moderate Present-Hedonistic
- Low Present-Fatalistic
- High Future
- Moderate Future-Transcendent

**中文:**

时间视角治疗模块提供六个维度的平衡时间视角发展的评估和干预：

- **过去积极**: 对过去的温暖、怀旧取向
- **过去消极**: 对过去的悲观、后悔取向
- **现在享乐**: 寻求快乐、冒险的现在取向
- **现在宿命**: 无助、绝望的现在取向
- **未来**: 目标导向、计划聚焦的取向
- **未来超越**: 对来世或长期遗产的取向 (2014 年添加)

**平衡时间视角剖面**:
- 高过去积极 (中等)
- 低过去消极
- 中等现在享乐
- 低现在宿命
- 高未来
- 中等未来超越

### 5. Temporal Depth Assessment v1.0 | 时间深度评估 v1.0

**Source | 来源**: SEP Temporal Consciousness + Psychoanalytic Temporal Depth + Mindfulness-Based Time Awareness + Phenomenological Depth Psychology

**English:**

Temporal Depth Assessment module evaluates the user's capacity to experience and integrate multiple temporal layers simultaneously:

- **Layer 1: Immediate Present**: Primal impression, sensory now
- **Layer 2: Recent Past/Future**: Retention and protention (seconds to minutes)
- **Layer 3: Extended Present**: Specious present (minutes to hours)
- **Layer 4: Biographical Time**: Personal history and anticipated future (days to years)
- **Layer 5: Generational Time**: Family history, cultural time, legacy (decades to centuries)
- **Layer 6: Deep Time**: Evolutionary, geological, cosmic time scales

**Depth Indicators**:
- Capacity to hold multiple temporal layers in awareness
- Integration of past traumas into present meaning
- Future orientation connected to present action
- Temporal flexibility (ability to shift temporal focus)
- Temporal coherence (narrative continuity across layers)

**中文:**

时间深度评估模块评估用户同时体验和整合多个时间层次的能力：

- **第 1 层：直接现在**: 原印象，感官现在

- **第 6 层：深层时间**: 进化、地质、宇宙时间尺度

**English:**

The self is not a static entity but a dynamic narrative construction unfolding across time. Key implications:

- Re-authoring life stories can transform self-experience

自我不是静态实体，而是跨时间展开的动态叙事建构。关键含义：

### 2. Balanced Time Perspective as Optimal Functioning | 平衡时间视角作为最佳功能

**English:**

Psychological well-being requires balanced orientation across temporal dimensions:
- Past-positive provides roots and continuity

- Balance is dynamic, not fixed

**中文:**

心理健康需要跨时间维度的平衡取向：
- 过去积极提供根基和连续性
- 未来提供方向和目的
- 现在享乐提供快乐和自发性
- 低过去消极和低现在宿命减少痛苦
- 平衡是动态的，非固定的
