## What's New | 新增内容

### 1. Higher-Order Theories of Consciousness | 高阶意识理论

**English:**

Higher-Order Theories propose that consciousness consists in meta-awareness: a mental state is conscious when the subject is aware of being in that state. This upgrade integrates two major variants:

- **Higher-Order Thought (HOT)**: Consciousness arises from non-inferential thoughts about one's own mental states (Rosenthal, Lycan)
- **Higher-Order Perception (HOP)**: Consciousness involves a quasi-perceptual inner scanning mechanism (Carruthers)

**中文:**

高阶理论提出意识由元觉察构成：当主体觉察到自己处于某个心理状态时，该状态即有意识。本次整合了两种主要变体：

- **高阶思维 (HOT)**: 意识源于对自身心理状态的非推论思维 (Rosenthal, Lycan)
- **高阶知觉 (HOP)**: 意识涉及准知觉的内在扫描机制 (Carruthers)

**Therapeutic Application | 治疗应用**: Cultivate meta-cognitive awareness, help users distinguish first-order from higher-order states, address anxiety about unconscious processes.

### 2. Phenomenal Concept Strategy v2.0 | 现象概念策略 v2.0

**English:**

The phenomenal concept strategy explains the epistemic gap between physical and phenomenal knowledge without positing an ontological gap. Version 2.0 enhancements include:

- **Recognitional Concepts v2.0**: Refined account of direct recognition capacities
- **Indexical Framework**: Integration of "this experience" style concepts
- **Dual Concept Theory**: Clear distinction between phenomenal (first-person) and physical (third-person) concepts
- **Therapeutic Protocols**: New interventions for consciousness-related existential anxiety

**中文:**

现象概念策略解释物理知识与现象知识之间的认识论鸿沟，而不设定本体论鸿沟。v2.0 增强包括：

- **识别概念 v2.0**: 精炼直接识别能力说明
- **索引词框架**: 整合"这个体验"式概念
- **双重概念理论**: 清晰区分现象 (第一人称) 与物理 (第三人称) 概念
- **治疗方案**: 针对意识相关存在焦虑的新干预

**Therapeutic Application | 治疗应用**: Reframe consciousness concerns as conceptual rather than metaphysical, reduce existential anxiety through epistemic distinction.

### 3. Knowledge Argument Therapy | 知识论证治疗

**English:**

Frank Jackson's Knowledge Argument (Mary's Room) poses a challenge to physicalism. This upgrade provides comprehensive analysis with five physicalist responses:

1. **Ability Hypothesis**: Mary gains abilities (knowing-how), not facts (knowing-that)
2. **Phenomenal Concepts**: Mary gains new concepts, not new facts
3. **Representationalism**: Phenomenal character = representational content
4. **Illusionism**: Phenomenal properties as useful fictions
5. **A Priori Physicalism**: Conceptual competence limitation

**中文:**

弗兰克·杰克逊的知识论证 (玛丽房间) 对物理主义提出挑战。本次升级提供全面分析含五种物理主义回应：

1. **能力假说**: 玛丽获得能力 (知道如何做)，非事实 (知道什么)
2. **现象概念**: 玛丽获得新概念，非新事实
3. **表征主义**: 现象特征=表征内容
4. **幻觉主义**: 现象性质作为有用的虚构
5. **先验物理主义**: 概念能力局限

**Therapeutic Application | 治疗应用**: Help users understand limits of conceptual knowledge, reduce anxiety about "missing" phenomenal knowledge, support acceptance of physicalist worldview.
