## Appendix: IIT 4.0 Overview | 附录：IIT 4.0 概述

### What is IIT? | 什么是 IIT？

**English:**
Integrated Information Theory (IIT) is a mathematical theory of consciousness that starts from phenomenology and derives mathematical postulates. It proposes that consciousness corresponds to integrated information (Φ) in a system. The higher the Φ, the richer the conscious experience.

**中文:**
整合信息理论 (IIT) 是一种意识的数学理论，从现象学出发推导数学后设。它提出意识对应于系统中的整合信息 (Φ)。Φ越高，意识经验越丰富。

### The Five Axioms | 五个公理

| Axiom | English | 中文 |
|-------|---------|------|
| 1 | **Intrinsic Existence**: Consciousness exists from its own perspective | **内在存在性**: 意识从其自身视角存在 |
| 2 | **Composition**: Consciousness is structured | **构成性**: 意识是有结构的 |
| 3 | **Information**: Consciousness is specific | **信息性**: 意识是特定的 |
| 4 | **Integration**: Consciousness is unified | **整合性**: 意识是统一的 |
| 5 | **Exclusion**: Consciousness has definite borders | **排他性**: 意识有确定的边界 |
