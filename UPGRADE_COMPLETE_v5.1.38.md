## Cross-Integration Highlights | 交叉整合亮点

### Culture × Positive Psychology | 文化 × 积极心理学

**English:**

- PERMA model culturally adapted: Relationship weight higher in collectivist cultures, Accomplishment weight higher in individualist cultures
- Character strengths expression varies by cultural context (e.g., humility more valued in Eastern cultures)
- Flow experience interpreted through independent/interdependent self-construal

**中文:**

- PERMA 模型文化适应：集体主义文化中关系权重更高，个人主义文化中成就权重更高
- 性格优势表达因文化情境而异（如谦逊在东方文化中更受重视）
- 心流体验通过独立/互依自我建构解释

### Digital × Emotion Regulation | 数字 × 情绪调节

**English:**

- Active technology use → Enhanced engagement and positive emotion
- Passive scrolling → Social comparison → Envy → Reduced well-being
- Digital detox → Mindfulness → Improved present-moment awareness
- FoMO → Anxiety pathway identified and targeted

**中文:**

- 主动技术使用 → 增强投入和积极情绪
- 被动滚动 → 社会比较 → 嫉妒 → 幸福感降低
- 数字排毒 → 正念 → 改善当下觉察
- FoMO → 焦虑路径识别并针对性干预

### Environment × Attention | 环境 × 注意力

**English:**

- Nature exposure → Soft fascination → Attention Restoration Theory
- Green space → Parasympathetic activation → Stress reduction
- Blue space (water) → Additional calming effects
- Environmental awe → Small self → Prosociality

**中文:**

- 自然暴露 → 柔和魅力 → 注意力恢复理论
- 绿色空间 → 副交感神经激活 → 压力减轻
- 蓝色空间（水）→ 额外镇静效应
- 环境敬畏 → 小自我 → 亲社会性

### Sleep × Emotion | 睡眠 × 情绪

**English:**

- Sleep deprivation → Amygdala hyperactivity → Emotional reactivity
- REM sleep → Emotional memory processing and integration
- Sleep quality → Next-day emotion regulation capacity
- CBT-I → Improved sleep → Enhanced emotional stability

**中文:**

- 睡眠剥夺 → 杏仁核过度活跃 → 情绪反应性
- REM 睡眠 → 情绪记忆加工和整合
- 睡眠质量 → 次日情绪调节能力
- CBT-I → 改善睡眠 → 增强情绪稳定性

### Nutrition × Mental Health | 营养 × 心理健康

**English:**

- Gut microbiota → Neurotransmitter production (serotonin, GABA, dopamine)
- Mediterranean diet → Anti-inflammatory → Reduced depression risk
- Omega-3 → Brain structure and function → Mood regulation
- Micronutrients → Neurotransmitter synthesis cofactors

**中文:**

- 肠道微生物群 → 神经递质产生（血清素、GABA、多巴胺）
- 地中海饮食 → 抗炎 → 降低抑郁风险
- Omega-3 → 大脑结构和功能 → 情绪调节
- 微量营养素 → 神经递质合成辅因子
