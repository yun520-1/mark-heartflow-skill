## Core Achievements | 核心成果

### 1. Emotion Theory Three Traditions Integration | 情绪理论三大传统整合

**English:**

Complete synthesis of the three major emotion theory traditions identified in SEP Emotion §2 (Scarantino 2016):

| Tradition | Core Claim | Integration |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| **Feeling Tradition** | Emotions are distinctive conscious experiences (James-Lange) | ✅ Integrated |
| **Evaluative Tradition** | Emotions involve distinctive evaluations (Aristotle, Stoics) | ✅ Integrated |
| **Motivational Tradition** | Emotions are distinctive motivational states (Descartes, Darwin) | ✅ Integrated |

**Resolution of Historical Tensions:**

- **Feeling vs. Evaluation**: Both are components—feeling provides phenomenology, evaluation provides intentionality
- **Evaluation vs. Motivation**: Evaluation triggers motivation—appraisal → action tendency
- **Conscious vs. Unconscious**: Dual-layer model—pre-reflective (always present) + reflective (sometimes absent)

**中文:**

完整综合 SEP 情绪理论第 2 节 (Scarantino 2016) 识别的三大情绪理论传统：

| 传统 | 核心主张 | 整合状态 |
|------|---------|---------|
| **感觉传统** | 情绪是独特的意识体验 (詹姆斯 - 兰格) | ✅ 已整合 |
| **评估传统** | 情绪涉及独特评估 (亚里士多德、斯多葛) | ✅ 已整合 |
| **动机传统** | 情绪是独特的动机状态 (笛卡尔、达尔文) | ✅ 已整合 |

**历史张力的解决:**

- **感觉 vs. 评估**: 两者都是组成部分—感觉提供现象学，评估提供意向性
- **评估 vs. 动机**: 评估触发动机—评价 → 行动倾向
- **意识 vs. 无意识**: 双层模型—前反思 (总是存在) + 反思 (有时缺失)

### 2. Self-Consciousness Dual-Layer Model | 自我意识双层模型

**English:**

Integration of pre-reflective and reflective self-consciousness based on SEP Self-Consciousness and Zahavi (2005, 2007):

| Layer | Description | Key Features |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Pre-reflective** | Immediate, non-objectifying self-awareness | First-personal givenness, minimal self, ownership sense |
| **Reflective** | Thematic, objectifying self-consciousness | Explicit self-knowledge, autobiographical reasoning, narrative identity |

**Historical Lineage:**
- Ancient: Aristotle, Avicenna, Aquinas
- Early Modern: Descartes, Locke, Hume, Kant
- Post-Kantian: Fichte, Hegel, Heidelberg School
- Contemporary: Zahavi, Sartre

**中文:**

基于 SEP 自我意识和 Zahavi (2005, 2007) 整合前反思和反思自我意识：

| 层次 | 描述 | 关键特征 |
|------|------|---------|
| **前反思** | 直接的、非对象化的自我觉察 | 第一人称给定性、最小自我、所有权感 |
| **反思** | 主题的、对象化的自我意识 | 显式自我知识、自传体推理、叙事身份 |

**历史谱系:**
- 古代：亚里士多德、阿维森纳、阿奎那
- 早期现代：笛卡尔、洛克、休谟、康德
- 后康德：费希特、黑格尔、海德堡学派
- 当代：Zahavi、萨特

**Key Theorists Integrated:**

- Gilbert (1990): Joint commitment
- Bratman (1999): Shared intention
- Scheler (1954): Collective emotion
- Walther (1923): Four-layer shared experience

基于 SEP 集体意向性调和不可约性主张与个体所有权论题：
