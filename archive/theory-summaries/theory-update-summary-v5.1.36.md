## Theoretical Foundations | 理论基础

### Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) | 认知行为疗法

**English:**

CBT is based on the cognitive model: the way individuals perceive a situation is more closely connected to their reaction than the situation itself (Beck 1979). Core principles:

1. **Cognitive Triad**: Negative views about self, world, and future
2. **Schema Theory**: Deep-seated cognitive structures organizing experience
3. **Automatic Thoughts**: Spontaneous cognitions influencing emotion
4. **Cognitive Distortions**: Systematic errors in thinking (all-or-nothing, catastrophizing, etc.)
5. **Behavioral Activation**: Engaging in valued activities to counteract depression

Integration with HeartFlow: CBT protocols are now mapped to predictive processing frameworks, where cognitive restructuring becomes prediction error minimization at the belief level.

**中文:**

CBT 基于认知模型：个体对情境的感知与其反应的联系比情境本身更紧密（Beck 1979）。核心原则：

1. **认知三元组**：对自我、世界和未来的负面看法
2. **图式理论**：组织经验的深层认知结构
3. **自动思维**：影响情绪的自发认知
4. **认知扭曲**：系统性思维错误（全或无、灾难化等）
5. **行为激活**：参与有价值的活动以对抗抑郁

与 HeartFlow 整合：CBT 协议现在映射到预测加工框架，认知重构成为信念层面的预测误差最小化。

### Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) | 接纳承诺疗法

**English:**

ACT is based on Relational Frame Theory (RFT) and functional contextualism. The Hexaflex model includes six core processes:

1. **Acceptance**: Willingness to experience difficult internal events
2. **Cognitive Defusion**: Changing relationship with thoughts (not content)
3. **Present Moment**: Flexible attention to here-and-now
4. **Self-as-Context**: Transcendent sense of self (observer perspective)
5. **Values**: Chosen life directions
6. **Committed Action**: Values-consistent behavior

Integration with HeartFlow: ACT's self-as-context aligns with Zahavi's minimal self and phenomenological givenness. Defusion techniques complement predictive processing by reducing over-weighting of high-level predictions.

**中文:**

ACT 基于关系框架理论（RFT）和功能性语境主义。六边形模型包括六个核心过程：

1. **接纳**：愿意体验困难的内在事件
2. **认知解离**：改变与思维的关系（而非内容）
3. **当下**：灵活关注此时此地
4. **自我背景**：超越性的自我感（观察者视角）
5. **价值**：选择的生活方向
6. **承诺行动**：符合价值的行为

与 HeartFlow 整合：ACT 的自我背景与 Zahavi 的最小自我和现象学给定性一致。解离技术通过减少高权重预测的过度加权来补充预测加工。

### Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) | 辩证行为疗法

**English:**

DBT integrates CBT with mindfulness and dialectical philosophy. Four skills modules:

1. **Mindfulness**: Core skills (what/how skills, non-judgmental stance)
2. **Distress Tolerance**: Crisis survival (TIPP, ACCEPTS, self-soothing)
3. **Emotion Regulation**: Understanding and changing emotions
4. **Interpersonal Effectiveness**: Relationship skills (DEAR MAN, GIVE, FAST)

Integration with HeartFlow: DBT's emotion regulation module extends Gross's process model with concrete skills. Mindfulness aligns with phenomenological reduction protocols.

**中文:**

DBT 整合 CBT 与正念和辩证哲学。四个技能模块：

1. **正念**：核心技能（什么/如何技能、非评判立场）
2. **痛苦耐受**：危机生存（TIPP、ACCEPTS、自我安抚）
3. **情绪调节**：理解和改变情绪
4. **人际效能**：关系技能（DEAR MAN、GIVE、FAST）

与 HeartFlow 整合：DBT 的情绪调节模块用具体技能扩展 Gross 的过程模型。正念与现象学还原协议一致。

### Compassion-Focused Therapy (CFT) | 慈悲聚焦疗法

**English:**

CFT is based on evolutionary psychology and affective neuroscience. Core model:

1. **Three-System Model**:
   - Threat System: Protection from danger (anxiety, anger)
   - Drive System: Pursuit of resources/goals (excitement, motivation)
   - Soothing System: Contentment, safeness, connection (calm, warmth)

2. **Compassionate Mind Training**: Developing compassion for self and others
3. **Shame Resilience**: Understanding and transforming shame
4. **Self-Compassion**: Treating oneself with kindness (Neff 2003)

Integration with HeartFlow: CFT's three-system model maps to predictive processing (threat = high prediction error, drive = reward prediction, soothing = precision optimization). Aligns with attachment theory (soothing system ↔ secure base).

**中文:**

CFT 基于进化心理学和情感神经科学。核心模型：

1. **三系统模型**：
   - 威胁系统：保护免受危险（焦虑、愤怒）
   - 驱动系统：追求资源/目标（兴奋、动机）
   - 安抚系统：满足、安全、连接（平静、温暖）

2. **慈悲心智训练**：培养对自己和他人的慈悲
3. **羞耻韧性**：理解和转化羞耻
4. **自我慈悲**：善待自己（Neff 2003）

与 HeartFlow 整合：CFT 的三系统模型映射到预测加工（威胁 = 高预测误差、驱动 = 奖励预测、安抚 = 精度优化）。与依恋理论一致（安抚系统 ↔ 安全基地）。
