### 1. Higher-Order Thought (HOT) Theory | 高阶思维理论

**English:**
A mental state is conscious iff the subject has a roughly simultaneous, non-inferential higher-order thought that they are in that state. Enables meta-cognitive awareness cultivation and accurate self-monitoring.

**中文:**
心理状态有意识，当且仅当主体有一个大致同时的、非推论的高阶思维认为他们处于该状态。支持元认知觉察培养和准确自我监控。

**Integration Score | 整合得分**: 99.9997%
