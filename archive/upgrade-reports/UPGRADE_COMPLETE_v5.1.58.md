**Source | 来源**: Seth (2013, 2021) + Critchley (2013, 2017) + Barrett & Simmons (2015) + SEP Interoception (2026)

**English:**

Models emotion as interoceptive inference—the brain's prediction and regulation of internal bodily states. Key components:

- **Interoceptive Prediction**: Brain predicts bodily needs before they arise
- **Allostatic Regulation**: Anticipatory body-state regulation (vs. reactive homeostasis)

- **Body-Brain Loop**: Continuous bidirectional prediction between body and brain
- **Clinical Applications**: Anxiety, depression, alexithymia intervention

**中文:**
焦虑建模为威胁相关预测误差的过度加权，对不确定性分配过度精度。干预：通过暴露、内感受训练和认知重构进行精度重新校准。
