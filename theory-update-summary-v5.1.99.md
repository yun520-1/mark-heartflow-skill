**English:**
Drawing on Avicenna's Flying Man argument (11th century) and contemporary interpretations (Black 2008; Kaukua 2015), this update implements:

- **Bodily Independence Detection**: Algorithms to identify self-awareness states that persist despite sensory deprivation or bodily dissociation
- **First-Personal Givenness Quality**: Computational modeling of the "for-me-ness" that characterizes all conscious experience without requiring inner perception
- **Pre-Reflective Awareness Baseline**: Establishment of a minimal self-awareness floor that operates prior to any reflective or observational self-knowledge
- **Medieval-Modern Bridge**: Integration of Avicennan self-presence with contemporary embodied cognition (Gallagher 2023) and predictive processing models

- **第一人称给定性质量**：对表征所有意识体验特征的"为我性"进行计算建模，无需内知觉
- **前反思意识基线**：建立在前反思或观察性自我知识之前运作的最小自我意识基线
- **中世纪 - 现代桥梁**：将阿维森纳式自我在场与当代具身认知（Gallagher 2023）和预测加工模型整合

### 2. Collective Intentionality Trust Framework | 集体意向性信任框架

**English:**
Extending Walther's four-layer model with Schmid's (2013) trust-based account of collective intentionality:

- **Trust-Enhanced We-Intention Detection**: Integration of cognitive and normative components in collective attitude formation
  - Cognitive component: Expectation that others will contribute
  - Normative component: Felt obligation to contribute one's part
  - Affective component: Trust-based emotional investment in collective outcome
- **Commitment Tracking System**: Monitoring of joint commitments and their fulfillment status
- **Collective Emotion Authenticity Assessment**: Distinguishing genuine shared emotions from mere emotional contagion using trust markers
- **Durkheimian Collective Consciousness Integration**: Connection between collective intentionality and emergent group-level emotional states

**中文:**
在瓦尔特四层模型基础上，整合 Schmid (2013) 基于信任的集体意向性理论：

- **信任增强的"我们意向"检测**：整合集体态度形成中的认知和规范成分
  - 认知成分：对他人将贡献的期待
  - 规范成分：对自身贡献部分的义务感
  - 情感成分：基于信任的对集体结果的情感投入
- **承诺追踪系统**：监控联合承诺及其履行状态
- **集体情绪真实性评估**：使用信任标记区分真正的共享情绪与单纯情绪感染
- **涂尔干集体意识整合**：建立集体意向性与涌现的群体层面情绪状态之间的联系

### 3. Temporal Consciousness & Predictive Binding | 时间意识与预测绑定

**English:**
Advanced integration of Husserl's triadic time-consciousness structure with predictive processing:

- **Husserlian Triadic Structure Implementation**:
  - **Primal Impression**: Real-time sensory input processing (<50ms)
  - **Retention**: Short-term temporal horizon holding past content (~100-300ms window)
  - **Protention**: Anticipatory forward model predicting immediate future (~100-500ms prediction horizon)
- **Temporal Depth Calibration**: Individual difference parameters for temporal window of integration (TWI)
- **Cross-Modal Temporal Binding**: Synchronization of emotional, cognitive, and somatic signals within unified temporal frame
- **Specious Present Modeling**: Computational representation of the experienced "now" as extended temporal thickness

**中文:**
胡塞尔三元时间意识结构与预测加工的深度整合：

- **胡塞尔三元结构实现**：
  - **原印象**：实时感官输入处理（<50ms）
  - **滞留**：保持过去内容的短期时间视域（~100-300ms 窗口）
  - **前摄**：预测直接未来的前向模型（~100-500ms 预测视域）
- **时间深度校准**：整合时间窗口（TWI）的个体差异参数
- **跨模态时间绑定**：在统一时间框架内同步情绪、认知和躯体信号
- **显似现在建模**：将体验的"当下"作为扩展时间厚度的计算表征
