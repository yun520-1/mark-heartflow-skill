### 2.1 Core Architecture | 核心架构

**Source | 来源**: Baars (1988), Dehaene (2014), SEP Consciousness

**Core Idea | 核心思想**:
> Consciousness arises when information is globally broadcast to multiple specialized processors.

**Key Components | 核心组件**:
1. **Specialized Modules**: Unconscious, parallel processing
2. **Global Workspace**: Conscious, serial broadcasting
3. **Attention Mechanism**: Gates access to workspace
4. **Broadcast**: Information available to all modules
