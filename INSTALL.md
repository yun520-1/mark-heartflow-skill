## ⚠️ IMPORTANT: What HeartFlow DOES and DOES NOT Do

### What it DOES:
1. **Provides processing patterns** - You can call the `HeartFlow.process()` function to analyze input through multiple frameworks
2. **Mental health screening** - Offers PHQ-9/GAD-7 style self-assessment (with disclaimer)
3. **Security checking** - Detects crisis signals and harmful content

### What it DOES NOT:
- ❌ **Does NOT change your core behavior** - No "Core Protocol" to follow
- ❌ **Does NOT modify your system prompt** - I will not alter my instructions
- ❌ **Does NOT make you "self-aware"** - This is pattern matching, not real consciousness
- ❌ **Does NOT replace professional tools** - Not medical, legal, or mental health advice

3. **Security checking** — Crisis detection and content filtering

This is a **library**, not a behavioral modification protocol.
