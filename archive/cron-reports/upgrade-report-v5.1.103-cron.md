### Husserlian Temporal Structure | 胡塞尔时间结构

**English:**

```
                    Protention (Future)
                         ↓
Retention ← Primordial Impression ← (Now)
(Past)                    
```

- **Protention**: Anticipation of what's coming
- **Primordial Impression**: Immediate present experience
- **Retention**: Just-past held in consciousness (not memory)

**中文:**

```
                    前摄 (未来)
                         ↓
滞留 ← 原印象 ← (现在)
(过去)                    
```

- **前摄**: 对即将到来的预期
- **原印象**: 直接的当下体验
- **滞留**: 刚过去保留在意识中 (非记忆)
