### 1. Panpsychism Fundamentals Module | 泛心论基础模块

**English:**

Panpsychism is the view that mentality is fundamental and ubiquitous in the natural world. This module integrates the core panpsychist framework including:

- **Micropsychism**: Mental properties are fundamental and instantiated by fundamental physical entities (quarks, electrons, etc.)
- **Cosmopsychism**: The cosmos as a whole is the fundamental conscious entity; individual consciousness derives from cosmic consciousness
- **Constitutive Panpsychism**: Macro-consciousness is constituted by micro-consciousness
- **Emergent Panpsychism**: Macro-consciousness emerges from micro-consciousness in non-standard ways

**中文:**

泛心论认为心智是自然界中根本且无处不在的。本模块整合了核心泛心论框架，包括：

- **微观泛心论**: 心智属性是根本的，由基本物实体（夸克、电子等）实例化
- **宇宙泛心论**: 宇宙整体是根本的意识实体；个体意识源自宇宙意识
- **构成性泛心论**: 宏观意识由微观意识构成
- **涌现性泛心论**: 宏观意识以非标准方式从微观意识涌现

**Key Philosophers | 关键哲学家**:
- Thales (泰勒斯) - First Western panpsychist
- Anaxagoras (阿那克萨哥拉) - "Everything is in everything"
- Spinoza (斯宾诺莎) - Dual-aspect monism
- Leibniz (莱布尼茨) - Monadology
- Schopenhauer (叔本华) - Will as inner nature
- William James (威廉·詹姆斯) - Neutral monism
- Whitehead (怀特海) - Process philosophy
- Galen Strawson (盖伦·斯特劳森) - Contemporary panpsychism
- Philip Goff (菲利普·戈夫) - Consciousness and fundamental reality

### 2. Russellian Monism Module | 罗素一元论模块

**English:**

Russellian Monism combines insights from Bertrand Russell's analysis of physical theory with panpsychist intuitions. Physical science describes the causal-relational structure of matter but not its intrinsic nature. The intrinsic nature of physical entities may be proto-mental or experiential.

**罗素一元论结合了伯特兰·罗素对物理理论的分析和泛心论直觉。物理科学描述物质的因果 - 关系结构，但不描述其内在本性。物理实体的内在本性可能是原心智的或经验的。**

**Key Components | 关键组件**:

| Component | English | 中文 |
|-----------|---------|------|
| **Categorical Properties** | Intrinsic nature of physical entities | 物理实体的内在本性 |
| **Dispositional Properties** | Causal-relational structure | 因果 - 关系结构 |
| **Proto-Consciousness** | Pre-experiential intrinsic nature | 前经验内在本性 |
| **Structural Knowledge** | What physics tells us | 物理学告诉我们的 |
| **Intrinsic Knowledge** | What we access via introspection | 我们通过内省获取的 |

**Key Philosophers | 关键哲学家**:
- Bertrand Russell (伯特兰·罗素) - The Analysis of Matter (1927)
- Arthur Schopenhauer (叔本华) - Will as intrinsic nature
- Thomas Nagel (托马斯·内格尔) - "Panpsychism" (1979, 1986, 1999, 2002)
- Galen Strawson (盖伦·斯特劳森) - "Realistic Monism" (2006)
- David Chalmers (大卫·查尔默斯) - "Consciousness and its Place in Nature" (2006)
- Philip Goff (菲利普·戈夫) - Consciousness and Fundamental Reality (2017)

### 7. Hard Problem Integration | 硬问题整合

**English:**

The hard problem of consciousness (Chalmers 1995) asks why and how physical processes give rise to subjective experience. Panpsychism and Russellian monism offer unique solutions by positing experience as fundamental.

**意识硬问题（查尔默斯 1995）询问物理过程为何及如何产生主观经验。泛心论和罗素一元论通过将经验视为根本的，提供独特解决方案。**

**Solution Framework | 解决方案框架**:
```
Hard Problem Solution (Panpsychist/Russellian):

1. Physical science describes structure, not intrinsic nature
2. Intrinsic nature may be proto-experiential
3. No "emergence" of experience from non-experience needed
4. Hard problem dissolves: experience is already fundamental
5. Combination problem replaces hard problem as central challenge
```
