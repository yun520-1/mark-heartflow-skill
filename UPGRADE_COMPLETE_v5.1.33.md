## 🎯 Key Enhancements | 关键增强

### 1. Self-Understanding Depth | 自我理解深度

**English:**

Users can now explore their self-experience at multiple levels:

- **Pre-Reflective Level**: Immediate, non-objectifying self-awareness
  - "I am aware of my pain without needing to identify myself"
  - First-person givenness, immunity to misidentification
  
- **Minimal Self Level**: Basic sense of ownership and agency
  - "This body is mine, this action is from me"
  - Ownership, agency, first-person perspective, phenomenal mineness
  
- **Narrative Self Level**: Extended self-constitution through life story
  - "My life has coherence and direction"
  - Temporal depth, identity continuity, redemption/contamination patterns

**中文:**

用户现在可以在多个层面探索自我体验：

- **前反思层面**：即时、非对象化的自我觉知
- **最小自我层面**：基本的所有权和能动性感受
- **叙事自我层面**：通过生命故事扩展的自我建构

### 2. Collective Emotion Recognition | 集体情绪识别

**English:**

System can now distinguish and work with different types of collective emotion:

- **Scheler-Type Direct Collective Feeling**: "We grieve together" without explicit coordination
  - Parents at child's deathbed
  - National enthusiasm (August Madness 1914)
  
- **Walther-Type Shared Experience**: Four-layer mutual awareness structure
  - Layer 1: Co-experience
  - Layer 2: Mutual empathy
  - Layer 3: Mutual identification
  - Layer 4: Mutual awareness
  
- **Durkheim-Type Mass Emotion**: Collective effervescence
  - Rituals, mass gatherings
  - Social facts bypassing individual psychology

**中文:**

系统现在可以区分和处理不同类型的集体情绪：

- **Scheler 型直接集体感受**
- **Walther 型共享体验**
- **Durkheim 型群体情绪**

### 3. Intersubjective Interventions | 主体间干预

**English:**

New intervention types now available:

- **We-Embodiment Exercises**: Synchronized movement, inter-bodily resonance
- **Shared Affordance Recognition**: "What does this environment offer us?"
- **Co-Enactment Facilitation**: Joint meaning-making through dialogue
- **Extended We-Agency Development**: Collective goal-setting, distributed responsibility

**中文:**

现在可用的新干预类型：

- **我们 - 具身练习**
- **共享可供性识别**
- **共同生成促进**
- **扩展的我们 - 能动性发展**
