## Theoretical Foundations | 理论基础

### 1. Emotion Theory: Three Traditions Integration | 情绪理论：三大传统整合

**Source | 来源**: SEP Emotion §2 (2025 Update) | SEP 情绪§2 (2025 更新)

**English:**

The SEP identifies three major philosophical traditions in emotion theory:

1. **Feeling Tradition (感受传统)**: Emotions as conscious experiences with distinctive phenomenology
   - James-Lange theory: emotions as perception of bodily changes
   - Phenomenological approach: emotions as felt bodily attitudes
   - Key insight: emotions have "what it's like" character

2. **Evaluative Tradition (评价传统)**: Emotions as evaluations or appraisals
   - Cognitive appraisal theory: emotions as evaluations of significance
   - Formal object theory: each emotion type has a core evaluation (fear→danger, anger→offense)
   - Key insight: emotions represent value properties

3. **Motivational Tradition (动机传统)**: Emotions as action tendencies or motivations
   - Action readiness theory: emotions prepare organism for action
   - Functional approach: emotions serve adaptive functions
   - Key insight: emotions motivate behavior

**Integration Challenge | 整合挑战**: Each tradition captures important aspects but faces limitations:
- Feeling tradition: struggles with unconscious emotions
- Evaluative tradition: struggles with emotionally irrational evaluations
- Motivational tradition: struggles with emotions without clear action tendencies

**v5.1.18 Solution | v5.1.18 解决方案**: Component-based integration with prototype structure
- Emotions as multi-component episodes with weighted contributions
- Prototype membership scoring (Fehr & Russell 1984)
- Contextual activation of different components

**中文:**

SEP 识别出情绪理论的三大哲学传统：

1. **感受传统**: 情绪作为具有独特现象学的意识体验
   - 詹姆斯 - 兰格理论：情绪作为身体变化的知觉
   - 现象学方法：情绪作为被感受的身体态度
   - 核心洞见：情绪具有"是什么感觉"的特征

2. **评价传统**: 情绪作为评价或评估
   - 认知评估理论：情绪作为重要性评估
   - 形式对象理论：每种情绪类型有核心评价（恐惧→危险，愤怒→冒犯）
   - 核心洞见：情绪表征价值属性

3. **动机传统**: 情绪作为行动倾向或动机
   - 行动准备理论：情绪为行动准备有机体
   - 功能方法：情绪服务于适应性功能
   - 核心洞见：情绪激励行为

**整合挑战**: 每种传统都捕捉到重要方面但面临局限：
- 感受传统：难以解释无意识情绪
- 评价传统：难以解释情绪性非理性评价
- 动机传统：难以解释没有清晰行动倾向的情绪

**v5.1.18 解决方案**: 基于成分的整合与原型结构
- 情绪作为具有加权贡献的多成分事件
- 原型成员评分（Fehr & Russell 1984）
- 不同成分的情境激活

### 2. Self-Consciousness: Dual-Path Model | 自我意识：双路径模型

**Source | 来源**: SEP Self-Consciousness §1-3 (2025 Update) | SEP 自我意识§1-3 (2025 更新)

**English:**

The SEP distinguishes two paths to self-knowledge:

1. **Intuitive Self-Knowledge (直觉式自我知识)**:
   - Non-inferential, immediate awareness of one's own mental states
   - Descartes' cogito: "I think, therefore I am" as indubitable foundation
   - Locke's "internal infallible Perception" of our own existence
   - Phenomenological givenness: pre-reflective self-awareness
   - Key feature: immunity to error through misidentification

2. **Inferential Self-Knowledge (推论式自我知识)**:
   - Based on observation, reasoning, and evidence
   - Aristotle's view: self-awareness through awareness of extra-mental objects
   - Aquinas's distinction: awareness of existence (immediate) vs. awareness of essence (inferential)
   - Key feature: fallible but can access deeper self-understanding

**Integration Challenge | 整合挑战**: How to reconcile immediate givenness with inferential depth?

**v5.1.18 Solution | v5.1.18 解决方案**: Dual-layer self-consciousness model
- Layer 1: Pre-reflective givenness (intuitive path) → sense of ownership, agency
- Layer 2: Reflective self-model (inferential path) → narrative identity, self-concept
- Dynamic interaction: intuitive grounding enables reflective exploration
- Prediction error signals trigger shifts between layers

**中文:**

SEP 区分了两种自我知识路径：

1. **直觉式自我知识**:
   - 非推论的、对自身心理状态的直接意识
   - 笛卡尔的 cogito："我思故我在"作为不可怀疑的基础
   - 洛克的"内部无误知觉"对自身存在的感知
   - 现象学给定性：前反思自我意识
   - 关键特征：对误认错误的免疫性

2. **推论式自我知识**:
   - 基于观察、推理和证据
   - 亚里士多德的观点：通过对外在对象的意识获得自我意识
   - 阿奎那的区分：存在意识（直接的）vs.本质意识（推论的）
   - 关键特征：可错但可获得更深层自我理解

**整合挑战**: 如何调和直接给定性与推论深度？

**v5.1.18 解决方案**: 双层自我意识模型
- 第一层：前反思给定性（直觉路径）→ 所有权感、能动性
- 第二层：反思自我模型（推论路径）→ 叙事身份、自我概念
- 动态交互：直觉基础使反思探索成为可能
- 预测误差信号触发层间转换

### 3. Collective Intentionality: Irreducibility Framework | 集体意向性：不可还原性框架

**Source | 来源**: SEP Collective Intentionality §1-2 (2025 Update) | SEP 集体意向性§1-2 (2025 更新)

**English:**

The central problem of collective intentionality:

**Irreducibility Thesis (不可还原性论题)**:
- Collective intentions are not just distributions of individual intentions
- "We intend to X together" ≠ "I intend to X + You intend to X + Common Knowledge"
- Collective intentionality requires genuine we-intentions

**Individual Ownership Thesis (个体所有权论题)**:
- Each individual has their own mind
- No collective super-brain exists
- All intentional states are had by individual brains

**Reconciliation Approaches | 调和方案**:

1. **Searle's We-Intention (Searle 的我们意向)**:
   - We-intentions are primitive individual intentional states
   - Content includes "we-mode" representation
   - Individual ownership preserved, collective content added

2. **Gilbert's Joint Commitment (Gilbert 的联合承诺)**:
   - Collective intentionality created by mutual agreement
   - Creates normative obligations between participants
   - "Pool of wills" model

3. **Bratman's Shared Intention (Bratman 的共享意向)**:
   - Interlocking individual intentions with mutual responsiveness
   - Subplan coherence requirement
   - Meshing subgoals

**v5.1.18 Integration | v5.1.18 整合**:
- We-Intention Detector module enhanced with three-theory synthesis
- Joint Commitment Assessment: normative obligation tracking
- Individual Ownership Preservation: no collective consciousness claims
- Collective Emotion Phenomenology: Scheler/Walther integration

**中文:**

集体意向性的核心问题：

**不可还原性论题**:
- 集体意向不只是个体意向的分布
- "我们打算一起做 X"≠"我打算做 X + 你打算做 X + 共同知识"
- 集体意向性需要真正的我们意向

**个体所有权论题**:
- 每个个体有自己的心智
- 不存在集体超级大脑
- 所有意向状态都由个体大脑拥有

**调和方案**:

1. **Searle 的我们意向**:
   - 我们意向是原始的个体意向状态
   - 内容包含"我们模式"表征
   - 保留个体所有权，添加集体内容

2. **Gilbert 的联合承诺**:
   - 集体意向性由相互协议创造
   - 在参与者之间创造规范性义务
   - "意志池"模型

3. **Bratman 的共享意向**:
   - 相互响应的交织个体意向
   - 子计划一致性要求
   - 交织子目标

**v5.1.18 整合**:
- 我们意向检测器模块增强，三理论综合
- 联合承诺评估：规范性义务追踪
- 个体所有权保留：无集体意识声称
- 集体情绪现象学：Scheler/Walther 整合

### 4. Emotion Prototype Structure | 情绪原型结构

**Source | 来源**: Fehr & Russell (1984) + SEP Emotion §1 | Fehr & Russell (1984) + SEP 情绪§1

**English:**

**Prototype Theory of Emotion Concepts**:
- Emotion categories are prototypically organized (not classical categories)
- Better and worse examples of emotions (fear is better example than awe)
- Borderline cases exist (is boredom an emotion?)
- Family resemblance structure (Wittgenstein)

**Five Components of Emotion Prototypes | 情绪原型五成分**:
1. **Phenomenology | 现象学**: What it feels like
2. **Physiology | 生理学**: Bodily changes
3. **Expression | 表达**: Facial/vocal display
4. **Appraisal | 评估**: Cognitive evaluation
5. **Action Tendency | 行动倾向**: Behavioral readiness

**Typicality Scoring | 典型性评分**:
- Each emotion instance scored on 5 components (0-1 scale)
- Overall typicality = weighted average
- Confidence assessment based on typicality score
- High typicality (>0.8): clear emotion categorization
- Medium typicality (0.5-0.8): moderate confidence
- Low typicality (<0.5): borderline case, uncertain categorization

**v5.1.18 Enhancement | v5.1.18 增强**:
- Emotion recognition confidence assessment
- Prototype network with similarity relations
- Emotional granularity mapping
- Contextual modulation of prototype weights

**中文:**

**情绪概念原型理论**:
- 情绪类别是原型组织的（非经典类别）
- 情绪有更好和更差的例子（恐惧是比敬畏更好的情绪例子）
- 存在边界案例（无聊是情绪吗？）
- 家族相似结构（维特根斯坦）

**情绪原型五成分**:
1. **现象学**: 感觉如何
2. **生理学**: 身体变化
3. **表达**: 面部/声音展示
4. **评估**: 认知评价
5. **行动倾向**: 行为准备

**典型性评分**:
- 每个情绪实例在 5 个成分上评分（0-1 量表）
- 整体典型性=加权平均
- 基于典型性评分的置信度评估
- 高典型性 (>0.8): 清晰情绪分类
- 中典型性 (0.5-0.8): 中等置信度
- 低典型性 (<0.5): 边界案例，不确定分类

**v5.1.18 增强**:
- 情绪识别置信度评估
- 具有相似性关系的原型网络
- 情绪粒度映射
- 原型权重的情境调节
