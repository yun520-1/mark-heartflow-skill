## 焦点理论整合 | Key Theoretical Integrations

### 1. 阿维森纳飞人论证 | Avicenna's Flying Man Argument

**English:**
- **Source**: Avicenna (1027), contemporary interpretation by Black (2008) and Kaukua (2015)
- **Implementation**: Non-observational self-knowledge module
- **Function**: Detects self-awareness states that persist without sensory input
- **Application**: Validates bodily-independent self-presence in clinical assessments

**中文:**
- **来源**: 阿维森纳 (1027)，Black (2008) 和 Kaukua (2015) 当代诠释
- **实现**: 非观察性自我知识模块
- **功能**: 检测无需感官输入仍持续的自我意识状态
- **应用**: 在临床评估中验证身体独立的自我在场

---

### 2. 瓦尔特集体情绪现象学 | Walther's Collective Emotion Phenomenology

**English:**
- **Source**: Gerda Walther (1923/2023), enhanced by Schmid (2013) trust framework
- **Implementation**: Four-layer shared experience model + trust-based commitment tracking
- **Function**: Distinguishes genuine collective emotions from mere contagion
- **Application**: Group therapy, team cohesion building, collective trauma processing

**中文:**
- **来源**: 格达·瓦尔特 (1923/2023)，Schmid (2013) 信任框架增强
- **实现**: 四层共享体验模型 + 基于信任的承诺追踪
- **功能**: 区分真正的集体情绪与单纯情绪感染
- **应用**: 团体治疗、团队凝聚力建设、集体创伤处理

---

### 3. 胡塞尔时间意识三元组 | Husserl's Time-Consciousness Triad

**English:**
- **Source**: Edmund Husserl (1991 critical edition), contemporary integration with predictive processing
- **Implementation**: Primal Impression / Retention / Protention computational model
- **Function**: Temporal binding for cross-modal emotion integration
- **Application**: Trauma processing (temporal fragmentation), mindfulness training, presence enhancement

**中文:**
- **来源**: 埃德蒙德·胡塞尔 (1991 评注版)，与预测加工的当代整合
- **实现**: 原印象/滞留/前摄计算模型
- **功能**: 跨模态情绪整合的时间绑定
- **应用**: 创伤处理（时间碎片化）、正念训练、在场感增强
