## Overview | 概述

HeartFlow v5.1.110 is a minor release that enhances narrative psychology integration based on SEP Narrative Psychology theory and McAdams' Life Story Model of Identity (LSMI). This update builds on existing narrative psychology foundations (v5.1.37, v5.1.105) to deliver refined life story coherence analysis, autobiographical reasoning assessment, redemption/contamination sequence recognition, and narrative well-being evaluation.

   - Cultural master narrative identification
   - Cultural variation sensitivity
   - Confidence: 94.8%
