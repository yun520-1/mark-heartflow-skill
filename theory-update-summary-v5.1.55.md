### 1. Phenomenal Concept Strategy v2.0 | 现象概念策略 v2.0

**Source | 来源**: SEP Physicalism §3.2, SEP Consciousness §4.1

**English:**

The Phenomenal Concept Strategy provides a physicalist response to the Knowledge Argument by explaining the epistemic gap without positing an ontological gap. This module integrates:

1. **Recognitional Concepts | 识别概念**: Phenomenal concepts as direct recognitional abilities
2. **Indexical Analysis | 索引词分析**: "This experience" as indexical reference
3. **Dual Concept Theory | 双重概念理论**: Physical vs. phenomenal modes of presentation
4. **Epistemic-Ontological Distinction | 认识论 - 本体论区分**: Gap is conceptual, not metaphysical

**Integration Framework | 整合框架**:
```
Physical Reality (本体：物理实在)
       ↑
       │ realized by (实现于)
       ↓
Phenomenal States (现象状态)
       ↑
       │ conceptualized via (通过...概念化)
       ↓
Phenomenal Concepts (现象概念)
       ↑
       │ deployed in (部署于)
       ↓
Introspective Knowledge (内省知识)

Key Insight | 关键洞见:
Mary gains new CONCEPTS, not new FACTS
玛丽获得新概念，非新事实
```

**Source | 来源**: SEP Qualia §3, SEP Physicalism §2.3

**English:**

Frank Jackson's Knowledge Argument (Mary's Room) challenges physicalism by arguing that complete physical knowledge leaves out phenomenal knowledge. This module provides complete analysis:

```

│ MARY'S ROOM (玛丽房间)                              │

│ - Mary: Brilliant color scientist | 杰出颜色科学家   │
│ - Environment: Black & white room | 黑白房间        │
│ - Knowledge: ALL physical facts | 所有物理事实      │

│ ARGUMENT | 论证                                      │
```
