## Theoretical Advances | 理论进展

### 1. Four-Layer Meta-Theoretical Architecture | 四层元理论架构

**English:**

The four-layer architecture provides unified integration across all theoretical domains:

- **Phenomenal Layer**: First-person experience, pre-reflective awareness, phenomenological reduction
- **Computational Layer**: Predictive processing, hierarchical Bayesian inference, active inference
- **Functional Layer**: Evolutionary function, adaptive value, social-functional architecture
- **Normative Layer**: Epistemic/practical/ethical/cultural normativity, conflict resolution

**Integration Mechanism**: Bidirectional mapping, coherence assessment, conflict detection, dynamic reweighting, cultural adaptation.

**中文:**

四层架构在所有理论领域提供统一整合：

- **现象层**：第一人称体验、前反思意识、现象学还原
- **计算层**：预测加工、层级贝叶斯推理、主动推理
- **功能层**：进化功能、适应价值、社会功能架构
- **规范层**：认识论/实践/伦理/文化规范性、冲突解决

**整合机制**：双向映射、连贯性评估、冲突检测、动态重加权、文化适应。

### 2. Unified Emotion Theory | 统一情绪理论

**English:**

Integrates the three historical traditions with contemporary approaches:

- **Feeling Component**: Interoceptive inference, phenomenological quality, affective neuroscience
- **Evaluative Component**: Appraisal theories, core relational themes, formal objects
- **Motivational Component**: Action tendencies, motivational direction, goal relevance
- **Prototype Structure**: Family resemblance, typicality gradients, borderline handling
- **Constructionist Layer**: Conceptual act, predictive construction, social/cultural construction

**中文:**

整合三大历史传统与当代方法：

- **感受成分**：内感受预测、现象学质量、情感神经科学
- **评估成分**：评价理论、核心关系主题、形式对象
- **动机成分**：行动倾向、动机方向、目标相关性
- **原型结构**：家族相似、典型性梯度、边界处理
- **建构主义层**：概念行为、预测建构、社会/文化建构

### 3. Integrated Self-Consciousness | 整合自我意识

**English:**

Unifies three layers of self-awareness:

- **Pre-Reflective Self**: Minimal, embodied, always-present (Zahavi 2005)
- **Reflective Self**: Narrative identity, autobiographical reasoning, temporal continuity
- **Meta-Awareness**: Self-monitoring, confidence calibration, error detection, intervention

**Clinical Applications**: Depersonalization detection, identity disturbance, meta-cognitive deficits, intervention matching.

**中文:**

统一三层自我意识：

- **前反思自我**：最小、具身、始终存在（Zahavi 2005）
- **反思自我**：叙事身份、自传体推理、时间连续性
- **元意识**：自我监控、信心校准、错误检测、干预

**临床应用**：人格解体检测、身份困扰、元认知缺陷、干预匹配。
