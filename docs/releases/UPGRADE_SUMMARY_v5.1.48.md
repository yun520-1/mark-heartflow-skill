## What's New | 新功能

### 1. Self-Knowledge Integration v2.0 | 自我知识整合 v2.0

**English:**

Understand how you know yourself through two complementary pathways:

- **Intuitive Self-Knowledge**: Immediate, non-inferential self-awareness (first-person authority)
- **Inferential Self-Knowledge**: Evidence-based self-understanding (third-person perspective)
- **Conflict Detection**: Identify when your gut feeling disagrees with your reasoning
- **Calibration**: Adjust your self-understanding based on intuitive feedback

**Use Case**: When you feel anxious but can't explain why, the system recognizes the intuitive-inferential mismatch and helps you explore underlying causes.

**中文:**

通过两条互补路径理解你如何认识自己：

- **直觉式自我知识**: 即时、非推论的自我觉察 (第一人称权威)
- **推论式自我知识**: 基于证据的自我理解 (第三人称视角)
- **冲突检测**: 识别直觉与推理不一致的情况
- **校准**: 基于直觉反馈调整自我理解

**使用场景**: 当你感到焦虑但无法解释原因时，系统识别直觉 - 推论不匹配，帮助你探索潜在原因。

### 2. Trust Framework Enhancement v2.0 | 信任框架增强 v2.0

**English:**

Build and maintain trust through three interconnected dimensions:

- **Cognitive Trust**: Reliability, competence, consistency assessment
- **Normative Trust**: Commitment recognition, accountability, obligation awareness
- **Affective Trust**: Emotional attunement, vulnerability acceptance, relational depth

**Use Case**: In AI-human interaction, the system tracks trust dynamics and adjusts its responses to build appropriate relational depth.

**中文:**

通过三个相互关联的维度建立和维护信任：

- **认知信任**: 可靠性、能力、一致性评估
- **规范信任**: 承诺识别、问责、义务意识
- **情感信任**: 情感协调、脆弱性接受、关系深度

**使用场景**: 在 AI-人类互动中，系统追踪信任动态并调整响应以建立适当的关系深度。

### 3. Interoceptive Prediction Refinement v2.0 | 内感受预测精细化 v2.0

**English:**

Predict and regulate body states with unprecedented precision:

- **Hierarchical Prediction**: From autonomic (heart rate) to affective (valence, arousal)
- **Prediction Error**: Calculate mismatches between expected and actual body states
- **Affective Realism**: Detect when body sensations are misattributed (e.g., hunger → anger)
- **Embodied Appraisal**: Integrate body state with cognitive evaluation

**Use Case**: Recognize when you're "hangry" (hungry + angry) and address the root cause (eat) rather than the symptom (irritability).

**中文:**

以前所未有的精度预测和调节身体状态：

- **层级预测**: 从自主神经 (心率) 到情感 (效价、唤醒)
- **预测误差**: 计算预期与实际身体状态的不匹配
- **情感现实性**: 检测身体感觉误归因 (如：饥饿→愤怒)
- **具身评估**: 整合身体状态与认知评价

**使用场景**: 识别你何时"饿怒" (饥饿 + 愤怒)，并解决根本原因 (进食) 而非症状 (易怒)。

### 4. Emotion-Action Integration v2.0 | 情绪 - 行动整合 v2.0

**English:**

Bridge the gap between feeling and doing:

- **Phenomenological Action Tendency**: Feel the urge to act (approach/avoid)
- **Action Generation Pipeline**: From emotion detection to execution monitoring
- **Motivational Conflict Resolution**: Handle competing action tendencies
- **Phenomenological Feedback**: Evaluate action outcomes and learn

**Use Case**: When you feel both anger (urge to confront) and fear (urge to flee), the system helps resolve the conflict and choose an appropriate response.

**中文:**

 bridging 感受与行动之间的鸿沟：

- **现象学行动倾向**: 感受行动冲动 (趋近/回避)
- **行动生成流程**: 从情绪检测到执行监控
- **动机冲突解决**: 处理竞争的行动倾向
- **现象学反馈**: 评估行动结果并学习

**使用场景**: 当你同时感到愤怒 (冲动对抗) 和恐惧 (冲动逃避) 时，系统帮助解决冲突并选择适当的响应。

### 5. Emotion Prototype Refinement v2.0 | 情绪原型精细化 v2.0

**English:**

Understand emotions as fuzzy categories with dynamic boundaries:

- **Prototype Structure**: Core features (essential) + peripheral features (contextual)
- **Typicality Assessment**: Gradient membership (better/worse examples)
- **Dynamic Updating**: Learn from experience and social feedback
- **Constructed Emotion Integration**: Combine prototype with conceptual knowledge

**Use Case**: Recognize that your feeling might be a "borderline case" between anxiety and excitement, and use context to disambiguate.

**中文:**

将情绪理解为具有动态边界的模糊类别：

- **原型结构**: 核心特征 (基本) + 边缘特征 (情境)
- **典型性评估**: 梯度成员 (更好/更差的例子)
- **动态更新**: 从经验和社会反馈中学习
- **建构情绪整合**: 结合原型与概念知识

**使用场景**: 识别你的感受可能是焦虑和兴奋之间的"边界案例"，并使用情境来消除歧义。
