## Cross-Cultural Well-Being Integration | 跨文化幸福感整合

### Cultural Adaptation of PERMA | PERMA 文化适应

**English:**

The PERMA model is adapted for cultural contexts:

**Individualist Cultures** (Western Europe, North America, Australia):
- **P** (Positive Emotion): Personal happiness, individual joy
- **E** (Engagement): Personal flow, individual mastery
- **R** (Relationships): Chosen relationships, friendship networks
- **M** (Meaning): Personal purpose, self-defined meaning
- **A** (Accomplishment): Individual achievement, personal goals
- Weighting: A > R > E > P > M

**Collectivist Cultures** (East Asia, Latin America, Africa):
- **P** (Positive Emotion): Relational harmony, family joy
- **E** (Engagement): Group flow, collective activities
- **R** (Relationships): Family bonds, community belonging (HIGHEST WEIGHT)
- **M** (Meaning): Family legacy, social contribution
- **A** (Accomplishment): Family honor, group achievement
- Weighting: R > M > A > E > P

**Assessment Adaptation**:
- PERMA-Profiler now includes cultural weighting adjustment
- Normative data stratified by cultural context
- Intervention recommendations culturally calibrated

**中文:**

PERMA 模型针对文化情境进行适应：

**个人主义文化**（西欧、北美、澳大利亚）：
- **P**（积极情绪）：个人幸福、个体喜悦
- **E**（投入）：个人心流、个体精通
- **R**（关系）：选择的关系、友谊网络
- **M**（意义）：个人目的、自我定义的意义
- **A**（成就）：个体成就、个人目标
- 权重：A > R > E > P > M

**集体主义文化**（东亚、拉丁美洲、非洲）：
- **P**（积极情绪）：关系和谐、家庭喜悦
- **E**（投入）：群体心流、集体活动
- **R**（关系）：家庭纽带、社区归属（最高权重）
- **M**（意义）：家庭传承、社会贡献
- **A**（成就）：家庭荣誉、群体成就
- 权重：R > M > A > E > P

**评估适应**：
- PERMA 评估仪现在包括文化权重调整
- 常模数据按文化情境分层
- 干预推荐文化校准

**English:**

**Active Use** (well-being enhancing):

- Information seeking (learning, research)

**Passive Use** (well-being diminishing):
- Endless scrolling

**Assessment Tools**:
- Technology Use Inventory (active vs. passive ratio)
- Social Media Use Integration Scale

- Problematic Internet Use Screening

**Intervention Pathways**:

- Passive use reduction
- Digital detox protocols

5. **CBT-I** (clinical insomnia): Full 6-session protocol

**Sleep-Emotion Integration**:
- Poor sleep → Amygdala hyperactivity → Emotional reactivity
- REM sleep → Emotional memory processing
- Sleep quality → Next-day emotion regulation capacity
- Intervention: Sleep optimization enhances emotion regulation

睡眠作为基础生物行为健康支柱整合：
