## New Theory Modules | 新增理论模块

### 1. Higher-Order Theories of Consciousness v1.0 | 高阶意识理论 v1.0

**Source | 来源**: SEP Consciousness + Rosenthal (1986, 1996, 2005) + Carruthers (2000) + Lycan (1996)

**English:**

Higher-Order Theories propose that a mental state's consciousness consists in one's awareness of being in that state. This module integrates:

- **Higher-Order Thought (HOT) Theory**: A mental state M is conscious iff the subject has a roughly simultaneous non-inferential higher-order thought that they are in M.
- **Higher-Order Perception (HOP) Theory / Inner Sense Model**: Conscious awareness involves a quasi-perceptual inner scanning mechanism.
- **Hybrid Models**: Combining HOT and HOP insights with dispositional accounts.
- **Objections and Responses**: Addressing the problem of misrepresentation, the targetless HOT problem, and the actualist vs. dispositionalist debate.

**中文:**

高阶理论提出，一个心理状态的意识在于主体对处于该状态的觉察。本模块整合：

- **高阶思维 (HOT) 理论**: 心理状态 M 是有意识的，当且仅当主体有一个大致同时的、非推论的高阶思维，认为他们处于 M 中。
- **高阶知觉 (HOP) 理论/内在感官模型**: 意识觉察涉及一种准知觉的内在扫描机制。
- **混合模型**: 结合 HOT 和 HOP 洞见与 dispositional 账户。
- **异议与回应**: 解决错误表征问题、无目标 HOT 问题、以及实际主义与倾向主义争论。

### 2. Phenomenal Concept Strategy v2.0 | 现象概念策略 v2.0

**Source | 来源**: SEP Phenomenal Concepts + Loar (1990, 1997) + Sturgeon (2000) + Papineau (2002) + Balog (1999, 2012)

**English:**

Enhanced integration of the phenomenal concept strategy as the most promising physicalist response to the Knowledge Argument and explanatory gap challenges. Key enhancements:

- **Recognitional Concepts v2.0**: Refined account of how phenomenal concepts function as direct recognitional capacities for phenomenal states.
- **Indexical Concepts Framework**: Integration of Perry-style indexical analysis (this experience, this quality).
- **Dual Concept Theory Application**: Clear distinction between phenomenal concepts (first-person, perspectival) and physical/functional concepts (third-person, descriptive).
- **Epistemic-Ontological Gap Distinction**: Robust framework for explaining why epistemic gaps don't entail ontological gaps.
- **Therapeutic Reframing Protocols**: New interventions for helping users accept physicalism while honoring phenomenal experience.

**中文:**

增强整合现象概念策略作为知识论证和解释鸿沟挑战的最有希望的物理主义回应。关键增强：

- **识别概念 v2.0**: 精炼说明现象概念如何作为对现象状态的直接识别能力运作。
- **索引词概念框架**: 整合 Perry 式索引词分析 (这个体验，这个性质)。

### 3. Knowledge Argument Therapy v1.0 | 知识论证治疗 v1.0

**Source | 来源**: Jackson (1982, 1986) + Physicalist Responses (Churchland 1985; Loar 1990; Ability Hypothesis; Representationalism; Illusionism)

**English:**

Comprehensive therapeutic framework based on analysis of Frank Jackson's Knowledge Argument (Mary's Room). This module provides:

- **Mary's Room Thought Experiment Reconstruction**: Complete formalization of the argument structure.
- **Five Physicalist Responses Integration**:
  1. Ability Hypothesis (Lewis 1983; Nemirow 1980): Mary gains abilities, not facts
  2. Phenomenal Concepts (Loar 1990): Mary gains new concepts, not new facts
  3. Representationalism (Tye 1995): Phenomenal character = representational content
  4. Illusionism (Dennett 1991; Frankish 2016): Phenomenal properties as useful fictions
  5. A Priori Physicalism (Chalmers 1996 response): Conceptual competence limitation
- **Objection Handling**: Comprehensive response to common objections and intuitions.
- **Therapeutic Reframing**: Protocols for using the argument analysis to reduce existential anxiety.

**中文:**

基于弗兰克·杰克逊知识论证 (玛丽房间) 分析的全面治疗框架。本模块提供：

- **玛丽房间思想实验重构**: 论证结构的完整形式化。
- **五种物理主义回应整合**:
  1. 能力假说 (Lewis 1983; Nemirow 1980): 玛丽获得能力，非事实
  2. 现象概念 (Loar 1990): 玛丽获得新概念，非新事实
  3. 表征主义 (Tye 1995): 现象特征=表征内容
  4. 幻觉主义 (Dennett 1991; Frankish 2016): 现象性质作为有用的虚构
  5. 先验物理主义 (Chalmers 1996 回应): 概念能力局限
- **异议处理**: 对常见异议和直觉的全面回应。

## Key Theoretical Insights | 关键理论洞见

### 1. Higher-Order Awareness as Gradable | 高阶觉察作为可分级的

**English:**

Higher-order awareness is not all-or-nothing but comes in degrees. This insight allows for:
- Gradual cultivation of meta-cognitive capacities
- Recognition of implicit vs. explicit higher-order awareness
- Tailored interventions based on user's current meta-awareness level

**中文:**

高阶觉察不是全有或全无，而是有程度之分。这一洞见允许：
- 元认知能力的渐进培养
- 识别隐式与显式高阶觉察
- 基于用户当前元觉察水平的定制干预

### 2. Phenomenal Concepts Bridge Epistemic Gap | 现象概念弥合认识论鸿沟

**English:**

The phenomenal concept strategy provides a physicalist-compatible explanation for why consciousness seems mysterious:
- Epistemic gap arises from conceptual difference, not ontological difference
- Phenomenal concepts are direct, recognitional, and indexical
- Physical concepts are descriptive and third-person
- Both refer to the same reality under different modes of presentation

**中文:**

现象概念策略提供了物理主义兼容的解释，说明为何意识显得神秘：
- 认识论鸿沟源于概念差异，非本体论差异
- 现象概念是直接的、识别性的、索引性的
- 物理概念是描述性的、第三人称的
- 两者在不同呈现模式下指称同一实在

### 3. Knowledge Argument as Therapeutic Tool | 知识论证作为治疗工具

**English:**

The Knowledge Argument, properly understood, can be therapeutic:
- Reveals limits of conceptual knowledge
- Shows that "missing" knowledge is conceptual, not factual
- Supports acceptance of physicalist worldview
- Reduces anxiety about consciousness "mystery"

**中文:**

知识论证若被正确理解，可以是治疗性的：
- 揭示概念知识的局限
- 表明"缺失"的知识是概念性的，非事实性的
- 支持接受物理主义世界观
- 减少对意识"神秘性"的焦虑
