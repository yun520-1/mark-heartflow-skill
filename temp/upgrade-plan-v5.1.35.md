**English:**
1. **Emotion Theory (SEP)**: Three traditions (Feeling, Evaluative, Motivational) with prototype structure (Fehr & Russell 1984)
2. **Self-Consciousness**: Pre-reflective vs reflective self-awareness (Zahavi), first-person givenness
3. **Collective Intentionality**: We-intention, joint commitment (Searle, Gilbert, Bratman), irreducibility to individual intentionality
4. **Embodied Cognition**: 4E framework (Embodied, Embedded, Enactive, Extended), ecological psychology, anti-computationalism
5. **Phenomenology**: Intentionality, first-person experience, life-world, temporal/spatial awareness

**中文:**
1. **情绪理论 (SEP)**: 三大传统（感受、评价、动机）与原型结构（Fehr & Russell 1984）
2. **自我意识**: 前反思与反思自我觉察（Zahavi），第一人称给定性
3. **集体意向性**: 我们意向、联合承诺（Searle, Gilbert, Bratman），不可还原为个体意向性
4. **具身认知**: 4E 框架（具身、嵌入、生成、扩展），生态心理学，反计算主义
