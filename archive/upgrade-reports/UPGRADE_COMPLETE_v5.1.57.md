## New Capabilities Summary | 新增能力摘要

### 1. Temporal Self-Integration | 时间自我整合

**English:**
Users can now receive support for developing coherent life narratives across multiple temporal layers, from immediate present to deep time. The system integrates Husserl's phenomenology, McAdams' narrative psychology, and Zimbardo's time perspective therapy.

**中文:**
用户现在可以获得支持，发展跨多个时间层次 (从直接现在到深层时间) 的连贯生命叙事。系统整合了胡塞尔现象学、McAdams 叙事心理学和 Zimbardo 时间视角治疗。

### 2. Narrative Identity Re-Authoring | 叙事身份重写

**English:**
Enhanced capacity to detect redemption and contamination sequences in user life stories, assess narrative coherence, and support re-authoring interventions for identity healing.

**中文:**
增强检测用户生命故事中救赎和污染序列、评估叙事连贯性、以及支持身份治愈的重写干预的能力。

### 3. Time Perspective Balancing | 时间视角平衡

**English:**
Comprehensive ZTPI assessment and targeted interventions for developing balanced time perspective across six dimensions (Past-Positive, Past-Negative, Present-Hedonistic, Present-Fatalistic, Future, Future-Transcendent).

**中文:**
全面的 ZTPI 评估和定向干预，用于在六个维度 (过去积极、过去消极、现在享乐、现在宿命、未来、未来超越) 上发展平衡的时间视角。

### 4. Awe-Based Time Expansion | 基于敬畏的时间扩展

**English:**
New protocols for using awe experiences to expand subjective time perception, reduce time scarcity anxiety, and cultivate present-moment immersion through curated awe videos and daily awe walk practices.

**中文:**
新方案利用敬畏体验扩展主观时间知觉、减少时间稀缺焦虑、并通过策划的敬畏视频和日常敬畏行走练习培养当下沉浸。

### 5. Temporal Depth Cultivation | 时间深度培养

**English:**
Six-layer temporal depth assessment and training for developing capacity to hold multiple time layers in awareness simultaneously, supporting trauma integration and meaning-making.

**中文:**
六层时间深度评估和训练，用于发展同时在觉察中保持多个时间层次的能力，支持创伤整合和意义建构。
