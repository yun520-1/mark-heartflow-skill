## Therapeutic Protocols | 治疗方案

### Protocol 1: Temporal Depth Cultivation | 时间深度培养

**Target | 目标**: Users with shallow temporal awareness, impulsivity, or temporal fragmentation

**Steps | 步骤**:
1. Guide user through 6-layer temporal awareness exercise
2. Start with Layer 1 (immediate present) - sensory awareness
3. Expand to Layer 2-3 (recent past/future, extended present)
4. Connect to Layer 4 (biographical time) - life story
5. Explore Layer 5-6 (generational, deep time) - legacy, cosmic perspective
6. Practice shifting awareness across layers (temporal flexibility)
7. Integrate insights into present-moment action

**Expected Outcome | 预期结果**: Enhanced temporal depth capacity, reduced impulsivity, increased meaning

### Protocol 2: Life Story Re-Authoring | 生命故事重写

**Target | 目标**: Users with identity fragmentation, low narrative coherence, or dominant contamination sequences

**Steps | 步骤**:
1. Conduct Life Story Interview (key scenes, characters, themes)
2. Identify redemption sequences (hidden or underdeveloped)
3. Identify contamination sequences (dominant negative patterns)
4. Support re-authoring of contamination sequences
5. Amplify and integrate redemption sequences
6. Cultivate autobiographical reasoning (connections between events and self)
7. Develop coherent life narrative with temporal continuity

**Expected Outcome | 预期结果**: Enhanced narrative coherence, stronger identity continuity, increased resilience

### Protocol 3: Time Perspective Balancing | 时间视角平衡

**Target | 目标**: Users with temporal imbalances (e.g., excessive future orientation, past-negative rumination, present-fatalistic helplessness)

**Steps | 步骤**:
1. Assess ZTPI six-dimension profile
2. Identify dominant and underdeveloped dimensions
