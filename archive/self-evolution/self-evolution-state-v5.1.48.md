## New Capabilities | 新能力

### Self-Knowledge Integration | 自我知识整合

**English:**

- **Intuitive Self-Knowledge Detection**: Recognize immediate, non-inferential self-awareness
- **Inferential Self-Knowledge Generation**: Evidence-based self-understanding
- **Conflict Detection**: Identify mismatches between intuitive and inferential self-knowledge
- **Calibration Protocol**: Adjust inferential based on intuitive feedback
- **First-Person Authority Assessment**: Evaluate confidence in self-ascriptions

**中文:**

- **直觉式自我知识检测**: 识别即时、非推论的自我觉察
- **推论式自我知识生成**: 基于证据的自我理解
- **冲突检测**: 识别直觉式与推论式自我知识的不匹配
- **校准协议**: 基于直觉反馈调整推论
- **第一人称权威评估**: 评估自我归属的信心

### Trust Framework | 信任框架

**English:**

- **Cognitive Trust Assessment**: Evaluate reliability, competence, consistency
- **Normative Trust Recognition**: Identify commitments and obligations
- **Affective Trust Detection**: Measure emotional attunement and vulnerability
- **Trust Dynamics Tracking**: Monitor trust evolution over time
- **Trust Repair Protocol**: Respond to trust violations appropriately

**中文:**

- **认知信任评估**: 评估可靠性、能力、一致性
- **规范信任识别**: 识别承诺和义务
- **情感信任检测**: 测量情感协调和脆弱性
- **信任动态追踪**: 监控信任随时间演变
- **信任修复协议**: 适当响应信任违规

### Interoceptive Prediction | 内感受预测

**English:**

- **Hierarchical Body State Prediction**: Multi-level physiological prediction
- **Prediction Error Calculation**: Compute sensory prediction errors
- **Affective Realism Detection**: Identify body-budget misattributions

- **Regulation Strategy Generation**: Body-based emotion regulation

- **层级身体状态预测**: 多层级生理预测
- **预测误差计算**: 计算感觉预测误差
