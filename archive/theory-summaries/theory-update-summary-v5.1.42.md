### 3. Digital Distress Detection | 数字困扰检测

**English:**

Recognizes technology-related psychological distress patterns:

**Technology Anxiety**:
- **Apprehension Patterns**: Worry about technology use + fear of inability to cope + technophobia indicators
- **FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)**: Social media checking compulsion + anxiety when disconnected + social exclusion fears
- **Update Stress**: Apprehension about software updates + change resistance + learning curve anxiety
- **Performance Anxiety**: Worry about digital performance + social media presentation stress + online judgment fears

**Doomscrolling Patterns**:
- **Compulsive Consumption**: Inability to stop scrolling + time loss awareness without behavioral change + continued use despite negative mood impact
- **Negative News Fixation**: Disproportionate attention to negative content + catastrophic thinking reinforcement + worldview distortion
- **Mood Deterioration Correlation**: Pre/post scrolling mood comparison + anxiety/depression symptom correlation + emotional exhaustion tracking
- **Disengagement Difficulty**: Failed attempts to stop + withdrawal-like symptoms + replacement behavior planning needed

**Notification Fatigue**:
- **Alert Overwhelm**: Stress response to notification sounds + constant checking behavior + inability to ignore alerts
- **Attention Fragmentation**: Task-switching frequency + deep work impairment + cognitive load from interruptions
- **Urgency Misperception**: All notifications treated as urgent + priority discrimination impairment + false urgency creation

**Digital Overwhelm**:
- **Information Overload**: Cognitive saturation from information volume + decision paralysis + analysis paralysis
- **Decision Fatigue**: Digital choice exhaustion + option overload + choice avoidance behaviors
- **Cognitive Exhaustion**: Mental fatigue from digital processing + recovery time needs + burnout indicators
