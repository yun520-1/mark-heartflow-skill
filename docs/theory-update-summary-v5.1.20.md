### 1.3 Collective Intentionality Phenomenology Enhancement | 集体意向性现象学增强 (96%)

**English:**

Integrates Walther's four-layer model and Scheler's collective emotion phenomenology:

**Walther's Four Layers of Shared Experience:**
1. **Co-experience**: A and B both experience x
2. **Empathic awareness**: A empathizes with B's experience of x
3. **Identification**: A identifies with B's experience
4. **Mutual awareness**: A and B are mutually aware of layers 1-3

**Scheler's Collective Emotion Types:**
- **Directly shared**: Grief at child's deathbed (immediate co-feeling)
- **Atmospheric**: National enthusiasm, crowd emotion (contagion-like)
- **Intentional**: Shared commitment to cause (we-intention based)

**中文:**

整合 Walther 的四层模型和 Scheler 的集体情绪现象学：

**Walther 共享体验四层：**
1. **共体验**: A 和 B 都体验 x
2. **共情觉察**: A 共情 B 对 x 的体验
3. **认同**: A 认同 B 的体验
4. **相互觉察**: A 和 B 相互觉察 1-3 层

**Scheler 集体情绪类型：**
- **直接共享**: 孩子临终前的悲伤 (直接共感)
- **氛围性**: 民族热情、群体情绪 (类似传染)
