- 自传体推理评估

### 3. Time Perspective Therapy v1.0 | 时间视角治疗 v1.0

**English:**
Balanced time perspective cultivation across six dimensions:
- Past-Positive (warm, nostalgic)
- Past-Negative (pessimistic, regretful)
- Present-Hedonistic (pleasure-seeking, spontaneous)
- Present-Fatalistic (helpless, hopeless)
- Future (goal-oriented, planning)
- Future-Transcendent (legacy, afterlife orientation)

**Target Profile**: High Past-Positive, Low Past-Negative, Moderate Present-Hedonistic, Low Present-Fatalistic, High Future, Moderate Future-Transcendent

**中文:**
在六个维度上培养平衡的时间视角：
- 过去积极 (温暖、怀旧)
- 过去消极 (悲观、后悔)
- 现在享乐 (寻求快乐、自发)
- 现在宿命 (无助、绝望)
- 未来 (目标导向、计划)
- 未来超越 (遗产、来世取向)

**目标剖面**: 高过去积极、低过去消极、中等现在享乐、低现在宿命、高未来、中等未来超越

Enhanced awe psychology with temporal applications:

- Curated awe video library (nature, art, music, science)
- Daily awe walk practice
- Awe-narrative integration (meaning-making)

增强敬畏心理学与时间应用：
- 时间知觉扩展 (对抗"时间稀缺"焦虑)
- 小我体验 (从自我转向广阔)
- 当下沉浸 (减少反刍和担忧)

**English:**
Six-layer temporal depth evaluation:

- Layer 4: Biographical Time (days-years)
- Layer 5: Generational Time (decades-centuries)
- Layer 6: Deep Time (evolutionary, cosmic scales)
