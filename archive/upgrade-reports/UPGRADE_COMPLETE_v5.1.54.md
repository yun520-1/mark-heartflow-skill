## Key Features | 关键特性

### 1. Hard Problem Framework | 困难问题框架

**English:**
- Distinguishes easy problems (functions, behaviors) from hard problem (subjective experience)
- Analyzes explanatory gap between physical and phenomenal facts
- Evaluates conceivability arguments (zombies)
- Addresses meta-problem (why we think there's a hard problem)

**中文:**
- 区分简单问题（功能、行为）与困难问题（主观经验）
- 分析物理事实与现象事实之间的解释鸿沟
- 评估可设想性论证（僵尸）
- 处理元问题（为何我们认为存在困难问题）

### 2. Illusionism Response | 幻觉主义回应

**English:**
- Dennett's heterophenomenology: third-person methodology
- Frankish's illusionism: phenomenal properties as introspective illusions
- User-illusion model: consciousness as simplified interface
- Quining qualia: denying four traditional qualia properties

**中文:**
- 丹尼特的异现象学：第三人称方法论
- 弗兰克的幻觉主义：现象性质作为内省幻觉
- 用户幻觉模型：意识作为简化界面
- 消除感受质：否认四种传统感受质属性

### 3. Phenomenal Concept Strategy | 现象概念策略

**English:**
- Epistemic gap ≠ ontological gap
- Recognitional concepts for experience
- Indexical analysis of phenomenal concepts
- Dual concept theory (physical + phenomenal modes)

**中文:**
- 认识论鸿沟 ≠ 本体论鸿沟
- 经验的识别概念
- 现象概念的索引词分析
- 双重概念理论（物理 + 现象模式）

### 4. New Mysterianism | 新神秘主义

**English:**
- McGinn's cognitive closure: human minds closed to consciousness-brain link
- Nagel's view from nowhere: objective-phenomenal divide
- Naturalistic dualism: consciousness natural but not reducible
- Humility position: acknowledging limits of understanding

**中文:**
- 麦金的认知封闭：人类心智对意识 - 大脑联系封闭
- 内格尔的无视角：客观 - 现象鸿沟
- 自然主义二元论：意识是自然的但不可还原
- 谦逊立场：承认理解的局限
